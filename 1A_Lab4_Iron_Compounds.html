Part 1: Detection of Fe²⁺ and Fe³⁺ Ions | Fe²⁺和Fe³⁺离子的检验

Materials and Equipment | 材料与仪器

Chemicals | 化学药品:

  • Iron(II) sulfate solution (FeSO₄) | 硫酸亚铁溶液
  • Iron(III) chloride solution (FeCl₃) | 氯化铁溶液
  • Sodium hydroxide solution (2M NaOH) | 氢氧化钠溶液（2M）
  • Potassium thiocyanate solution (KSCN) | 硫氰化钾溶液
  • Potassium ferricyanide solution K₃[Fe(CN)₆] | 铁氰化钾溶液

Equipment | 实验仪器:

  • Test tubes (6) | 试管（6支）
  • Dropper pipettes | 胶头滴管
  • Test tube rack | 试管架

Procedure | 操作步骤

Step 1 | 步骤1:

  • Pour 2 mL of FeSO₄ solution into three test tubes, and 2 mL of FeCl₃ solution into another three test tubes.
  • 向三支试管中分别加入2毫升FeSO₄溶液，向另三支试管中分别加入2毫升FeCl₃溶液。

Step 2 | 步骤2:

  • Add 3-4 drops of NaOH solution to the first tube of each set.
  • 向每组的第一支试管中加入3-4滴NaOH溶液。
  • Observe the color of precipitates formed.
  • 观察生成沉淀的颜色。
  • Let stand and observe color changes over time.
  • 静置并观察颜色随时间的变化。

Step 3 | 步骤3:

  • Add 2-3 drops of KSCN solution to the second tube of each set.
  • 向每组的第二支试管中加入2-3滴KSCN溶液。
  • Observe color changes.
  • 观察颜色变化。

Step 4 | 步骤4:

  • Add 2-3 drops of K₃[Fe(CN)₆] solution to the third tube of each set.
  • 向每组的第三支试管中加入2-3滴K₃[Fe(CN)₆]溶液。
  • Observe for precipitate formation.
  • 观察是否有沉淀生成。

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Iron(II) Compounds | 亚铁化合物

Iron(II) Oxide (FeO) | 氧化亚铁:

  • Black solid (黑色固体)
  • Basic (碱性) oxide (碱性氧化物)
  • Unstable, easily oxidized (不稳定，易被氧化)

Iron(II) Hydroxide [Fe(OH)₂] | 氢氧化亚铁:

Preparation | 制备:


FeSO₄ + 2NaOH → Fe(OH)₂↓ + Na₂SO₄

Properties | 性质:

  • White precipitate when freshly formed (新制时为白色沉淀)
  • Rapidly turns gray-green, then brown in air (在空气中迅速变灰绿色，然后变棕色)
  • Oxidation (氧化) process (氧化过程):
    • 
4Fe(OH)₂ + O₂ + 2H₂O → 4Fe(OH)₃

2. Iron(III) Compounds | 铁化合物

Iron(III) Oxide (Fe₂O₃) | 三氧化二铁:

  • Red-brown solid (红棕色固体)
  • Main component of rust (铁锈的主要成分)
  • Amphoteric (两性) oxide (两性氧化物)

As a base | 作为碱:


Fe₂O₃ + 6HCl → 2FeCl₃ + 3H₂O

As an acid | 作为酸:


Fe₂O₃ + 2NaOH --熔融--> 2NaFeO₂ + H₂O

Iron(III) Hydroxide [Fe(OH)₃] | 氢氧化铁:

Preparation | 制备:


FeCl₃ + 3NaOH → Fe(OH)₃↓ + 3NaCl

Properties | 性质:

  • Red-brown precipitate (红棕色沉淀)
  • Amphoteric (两性) hydroxide (两性氢氧化物)
  • Thermal decomposition (热分解):
    • 
2Fe(OH)₃ --Δ--> Fe₂O₃ + 3H₂O

3. Mixed Oxide: Fe₃O₄ | 混合氧化物：四氧化三铁

Composition | 组成:

  • Can be written as FeO·Fe₂O₃
  • Contains both Fe²⁺ and Fe³⁺ (同时含有Fe²⁺和Fe³⁺)

Properties | 性质:

  • Black solid (黑色固体)
  • Magnetic (有磁性)
  • Natural form: magnetite (天然形式：磁铁矿)

Preparation | 制备:


3Fe + 2O₂ --Δ--> Fe₃O₄
3Fe + 4H₂O(g) --Δ--> Fe₃O₄ + 4H₂↑

4. Detection of Fe²⁺ and Fe³⁺ | Fe²⁺和Fe³⁺的检验

Testing for Fe²⁺ | 检验Fe²⁺:

Method 1: With NaOH | 用NaOH:


Fe²⁺ + 2OH⁻ → Fe(OH)₂↓ (white, turns gray-green)

Method 2: With K₃[Fe(CN)₆] | 用铁氰化钾:


3Fe²⁺ + 2[Fe(CN)₆]³⁻ → Fe₃[Fe(CN)₆]₂↓ (dark blue precipitate)

Testing for Fe³⁺ | 检验Fe³⁺:

Method 1: With NaOH | 用NaOH:


Fe³⁺ + 3OH⁻ → Fe(OH)₃↓ (red-brown)

Method 2: With KSCN | 用硫氰化钾:


Fe³⁺ + 3SCN⁻ → Fe(SCN)₃ (blood-red solution)

Method 3: With K₄[Fe(CN)₆] | 用亚铁氰化钾:


4Fe³⁺ + 3[Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻ → Fe₄[Fe(CN)₆]₃↓ (blue precipitate, Prussian blue)

5. Applications | 应用

Fe₂O₃:

  • Red pigment (红色颜料)
  • Polishing agent (抛光剂)
  • Catalyst (催化剂)

Fe₃O₄:

  • Magnetic materials (磁性材料)
  • Black pigment (黑色颜料)
  • Catalyst (催化剂)

FeSO₄:

  • Water treatment (水处理)
  • Nutritional supplement (营养补充剂)
  • Reducing agent (还原剂)
