Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Physical Properties | 铝的物理性质

Appearance | 外观:
  • Silvery-white metal with metallic luster (银白色金属，具有金属光泽)
  • Soft and lightweight (软而轻)
  • Malleable and ductile (可锻可延展)
Density | 密度:
  • 2.70 g/cm³ (low density for a metal) (金属中密度较低)
Melting Point | 熔点:
  • 660°C (relatively low) (相对较低)
Conductivity | 导电性:
  • Excellent conductor of heat and electricity (优良的热和电导体)
  • Second only to copper in electrical conductivity (导电性仅次于铜)

2. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Reaction with Oxygen | 与氧气反应

At Room Temperature | 室温下: 
4Al + 3O₂ → 2Al₂O₃ (thin protective layer)
  • Forms dense protective oxide layer (形成致密的保护性氧化层)
  • Prevents further oxidation (阻止进一步氧化)
  • This is why aluminum is corrosion-resistant (这就是铝耐腐蚀的原因)
When Heated | 加热时: 
4Al + 3O₂ --Δ--> 2Al₂O₃ (vigorous reaction)
  • Bright white flame (明亮的白色火焰)
  • Releases large amount of heat (释放大量热)

(2) Reaction with Acids | 与酸反应

With Dilute Hydrochloric Acid | 与稀盐酸: 
2Al + 6HCl → 2AlCl₃ + 3H₂↑
With Dilute Sulfuric Acid | 与稀硫酸: 
2Al + 3H₂SO₄(dilute) → Al₂(SO₄)₃ + 3H₂↑
With Concentrated Nitric Acid (Cold) | 与浓硝酸（冷）:
  • Aluminum is passivated (铝被钝化)
  • Forms protective Al₂O₃ layer (形成保护性Al₂O₃层)
  • NO reaction at room temperature (室温下不反应)

(3) Reaction with Bases | 与碱反应

Amphoteric Nature | 两性: Aluminum is amphoteric - reacts with both acids AND bases! 铝是两性的 - 既与酸反应又与碱反应！ With Sodium Hydroxide Solution | 与氢氧化钠溶液: 
2Al + 2NaOH + 2H₂O → 2NaAlO₂ + 3H₂↑
  • Produces hydrogen gas (产生氢气)
  • Forms sodium aluminate (形成偏铝酸钠)
  • Solution remains clear (溶液保持澄清)

(4) Thermite Reaction | 铝热反应

Principle | 原理: Aluminum is a strong reducing agent at high temperature 铝在高温下是强还原剂 Example: Reduction of Fe₂O₃ | 示例：还原Fe₂O₃: 
2Al + Fe₂O₃ --Δ--> 2Fe + Al₂O₃ (highly exothermic)
  • Temperature reaches ~3000°C (温度达到约3000°C)
  • Molten iron produced (产生熔融铁)
  • Used for welding rails (用于焊接铁轨)
Other Thermite Reactions | 其他铝热反应: 
2Al + 3CuO --Δ--> 3Cu + Al₂O₃
8Al + 3Fe₃O₄ --Δ--> 9Fe + 4Al₂O₃

3. Aluminum Oxide (Al₂O₃) | 氧化铝

Properties | 性质:
  • White solid (白色固体)
  • Very high melting point: 2054°C (熔点很高：2054°C)
  • Amphoteric (两性) oxide (两性氧化物)
  • Insoluble (不溶) in water (不溶于水)
Amphoteric Reactions | 两性反应: As a base (with acids) | 作为碱（与酸）: 
Al₂O₃ + 6HCl → 2AlCl₃ + 3H₂O
Al₂O₃ + 3H₂SO₄ → Al₂(SO₄)₃ + 3H₂O
As an acid (with bases) | 作为酸（与碱）: 
Al₂O₃ + 2NaOH → 2NaAlO₂ + H₂O (molten)
Al₂O₃ + 2NaOH + 3H₂O → 2Na[Al(OH)₄] (solution)

4. Uses of Aluminum | 铝的用途

1. Construction and Transportation | 建筑和运输:
  • Aircraft and spacecraft (飞机和航天器)
  • Automobile parts (汽车零件)
  • Building materials (建筑材料)
  • Lightweight and strong (轻而强)
2. Electrical Applications | 电气应用:
  • Electrical wiring (电线)
  • Power transmission lines (输电线)
  • Good conductor, lightweight (良好导体，重量轻)
3. Packaging | 包装:
  • Aluminum foil (铝箔)
  • Beverage cans (饮料罐)
  • Food containers (食品容器)
4. Alloys | 合金:
  • Duralumin (Al-Cu-Mg-Mn) (硬铝)
  • Used in aircraft construction (用于飞机制造)
5. Thermite Welding | 铝热焊接:
  • Welding railway tracks (焊接铁轨)
  • Repairing large metal parts (修复大型金属部件)
