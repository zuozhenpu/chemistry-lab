  • - 用向下排水法收集

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now let's understand the principles behind your observations.

现在让我们理解你观察背后的原理。

1. Preparation of Methane | 甲烷的制备

Chemical Reaction | 化学方程式:


CH₃COONa + NaOH --CaO, Δ--> CH₄↑ + Na₂CO₃

Role of Reagents | 试剂作用:

  • CH₃COONa: Source of methyl group (CH₃-) (甲基来源)
  • NaOH: Provides OH⁻ for reaction (提供OH⁻参与反应)
  • CaO: Absorbs water, prevents hydrolysis (吸水，防止水解)

Why Soda Lime? | 为什么用碱石灰？

  • NaOH alone would cause sodium acetate to hydrolyze
  • 单独NaOH会导致醋酸钠水解
  • CaO keeps mixture dry
  • CaO保持混合物干燥

2. Molecular Structure | 分子结构

Molecular Formula | 分子式: CH₄

3D Structure | 三维结构:

  • Tetrahedral geometry (正四面体几何)
  • Bond angle: 109.5° (键角：109.5°)
  • All C-H bonds are equivalent (所有C-H键等同)
  • sp³ hybridization (sp³杂化)

3. Physical Properties | 物理性质

  • Colorless, odorless gas (无色无味气体)
  • Less dense than air (比空气密度小)
  • Sparingly soluble (微溶) in water (微溶于水)
  • Boiling point: -161.5°C (沸点：-161.5°C)

4. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Stability | 稳定性

At Room Temperature | 室温下:

  • Does NOT react with strong oxidizers (不与强氧化剂反应)
  • Bromine water: NO decolorization (溴水：不褪色)
  • KMnO₄: NO decolorization (KMnO₄：不褪色)

Reason | 原因:

  • Strong C-H bonds (bond energy = 413 kJ/mol)
  • 强C-H键（键能 = 413 kJ/mol）
  • Stable tetrahedral structure
  • 稳定的四面体结构
  • No double bonds to react
  • 没有双键可反应

(2) Combustion | 燃烧

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


CH₄ + 2O₂ → CO₂ + 2H₂O

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Pale blue flame (淡蓝色火焰)
  • No smoke (clean combustion) (无烟（完全燃烧）)
  • Releases large amount of heat (890 kJ/mol)
  • 释放大量热（890 kJ/mol）
  • Water condenses on cold surface (水在冷表面凝结)
  • CO₂ turns limewater milky (CO₂使石灰水变浑浊)

Incomplete Combustion | 不完全燃烧:


2CH₄ + 3O₂ → 2CO + 4H₂O
  • Occurs when oxygen is insufficient (氧气不足时发生)
  • Produces toxic CO (产生有毒CO)

(3) Substitution Reaction with Chlorine | 与氯气的取代反应

Step-wise Reactions | 逐步反应:


CH₄ + Cl₂ --light--> CH₃Cl + HCl (chloromethane)
CH₃Cl + Cl₂ --light--> CH₂Cl₂ + HCl (dichloromethane)
CH₂Cl₂ + Cl₂ --light--> CHCl₃ + HCl (chloroform)
CHCl₃ + Cl₂ --light--> CCl₄ + HCl (carbon tetrachloride)

Mechanism | 机理: Free radical substitution

自由基取代

Steps | 步骤:

1. Initiation: Cl₂ --light--> 2Cl· (引发)

2. Propagation: CH₄ + Cl· → CH₃· + HCl (链传递)

CH₃· + Cl₂ → CH₃Cl + Cl·

3. Termination: Cl· + Cl· → Cl₂ (链终止)

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Yellow-green Cl₂ color fades (黄绿色Cl₂褪去)
  • Oily liquid droplets = CH₂Cl₂, CHCl₃, CCl₄ (油状液滴 = CH₂Cl₂, CHCl₃, CCl₄)
  • Pungent odor (刺激性气味) = HCl gas (刺激性气味 = HCl气体)
  • Light is necessary to break Cl-Cl bond (光是打破Cl-Cl键所必需的)

5. Comparison: Methane vs. Ethylene | 对比：甲烷与乙烯

| Property | Methane (CH₄) | Ethylene (C₂H₄) |

|----------|---------------|-----------------|

| Structure | Tetrahedral, sp³ | Planar, sp² |

| Saturation | Saturated | Unsaturated |

| Main reaction | Substitution | Addition |

| With Br₂ | No reaction | Decolorizes |

| With KMnO₄ | No reaction | Decolorizes |

| Combustion | Pale blue, no smoke | Bright, smoky |

| Reactivity | Stable | Reactive |

I. Alkanes (Saturated Hydrocarbons) | 烷烃（饱和烃）

General Formula | 通式: CₙH₂ₙ₊₂ (n ≥ 1)

Characteristics | 特征:

  • Only C-C single bonds and C-H bonds (只有C-C单键和C-H键)
  • Saturated with hydrogen (氢饱和)
  • Relatively stable (相对稳定)
  • Main reaction: substitution (主要反应：取代)

II. Properties of Methane | 甲烷的性质

Physical | 物理:

  • Simplest alkane (最简单的烷烃)
  • Colorless, odorless gas (无色无味气体)
  • Less dense than air (比空气密度小)

Chemical | 化学:

  • Stable at room temperature (室温下稳定)
  • Burns with pale blue flame (淡蓝色火焰燃烧)
  • Substitution with halogens under light (光照下与卤素取代)

III. Important Concepts | 重要概念

Mine Gas (Coal Mine Methane) | 瓦斯（煤矿甲烷）:

  • Methane in coal mines (煤矿中的甲烷)
  • Explosive (爆炸性) range: 5-15% in air (爆炸范围：空气中5-15%)
  • Safety measures: ventilation, no sparks
  • 安全措施：通风，无火花
