Safety Guidelines | 安全指南

After considering the questions above, review these safety guidelines:

⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS | 重要安全警告

1. Chlorine is TOXIC | 氯气有毒

  • - This experiment MUST be conducted in a fume hood
  • - 本实验必须在通风橱中进行
  • - Never smell chlorine gas directly
  • - 绝不可直接闻氯气

2. Concentrated Acids | 浓酸处理

  • - Wear safety goggles and gloves at all times
  • - 始终佩戴护目镜和手套
  • - Add acid slowly along the wall of the flask
  • - 沿烧瓶壁缓慢加入酸

3. Gas Absorption | 尾气吸收

  • - All waste chlorine gas must be absorbed by NaOH solution
  • - 所有废氯气必须用NaOH溶液吸收
  • - Never release chlorine gas into the atmosphere
  • - 绝不可将氯气直接排放到大气中

4. Emergency Procedures | 应急程序

  • - If chlorine is inhaled: Move to fresh air immediately and seek medical attention
  • - 如吸入氯气：立即转移到通风处并就医
  • - If acid contacts skin: Rinse with large amounts of water for at least 15 minutes
  • - 如酸接触皮肤：用大量水冲洗至少15分钟

Experimental Procedure | 实验操作

Inquiry-Based Learning | 探究式学习
During the experiment, focus on careful observation of phenomena and think about the questions provided.
在实验过程中，请仔细观察现象并思考提供的问题。

Part 1: Apparatus Setup | 装置组装

Materials and Equipment | 材料与仪器

Chemicals | 化学药品:

  • Manganese dioxide (MnO₂) powder, 5 g | 二氧化锰粉末，5克
  • Concentrated hydrochloric acid (HCl, 37%), 20 mL | 浓盐酸（37%），20毫升
  • Saturated sodium chloride solution (NaCl), 100 mL | 饱和食盐水，100毫升
  • Concentrated sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄, 98%), 50 mL | 浓硫酸（98%），50毫升
  • Sodium hydroxide solution (NaOH, 2 M), 200 mL | 氢氧化钠溶液（2 M），200毫升
  • Bromine water (Br₂/H₂O) | 溴水
  • Potassium permanganate solution (KMnO₄, acidified) | 酸性高锰酸钾溶液
  • Copper wire | 铜丝
  • Colored cloth strips | 有色布条
  • Starch-potassium iodide test paper | 淀粉碘化钾试纸

Equipment | 实验仪器:

  • Round-bottom flask (250 mL) | 圆底烧瓶（250毫升）
  • Separatory funnel | 分液漏斗
  • Alcohol lamp | 酒精灯
  • Gas delivery tubes | 导气管
  • Wash bottles (3) | 洗气瓶（3个）
  • Gas collection bottles (3) | 集气瓶（3个）
  • Beaker (500 mL) for tail gas absorption | 烧杯（500毫升）用于尾气吸收
  • Iron stand with clamps | 铁架台和铁夹
  • Test tubes | 试管
  • Thermometer | 温度计

Procedure | 操作步骤

1. Assemble the gas generation apparatus | 组装气体发生装置

  • - Place 5.0 g of MnO₂ powder in the round-bottom flask
  • - 在圆底烧瓶中加入5.0克二氧化锰粉末
  • - Insert the separatory funnel into the flask stopper
  • - 将分液漏斗插入烧瓶塞中

2. Connect the purification system | 连接净化系统

  • - Connect gas delivery tube to the first wash bottle containing 100 mL saturated NaCl solution
  • - 将导气管连接到第一个洗气瓶（装有100毫升饱和食盐水）
  • - Connect to the second wash bottle containing 50 mL concentrated H₂SO₄
  • - 连接到第二个洗气瓶（装有50毫升浓硫酸）

3. Set up tail gas absorption | 设置尾气吸收

  • - Place a 500 mL beaker containing 200 mL of 2 M NaOH solution at the end
  • - 在末端放置装有200毫升2 M NaOH溶液的500毫升烧杯
  • - Ensure the gas delivery tube extends into the NaOH solution
  • - 确保导气管伸入NaOH溶液中

4. Check for leaks | 检查气密性

  • - Close the separatory funnel stopcock
  • - 关闭分液漏斗活塞
  • - Gently warm the flask and observe if bubbles appear in the NaOH solution
  • - 轻轻加热烧瓶，观察NaOH溶液中是否出现气泡

Inquiry Questions | 探究问题

1. Why is it important to check the apparatus for leaks before starting?

为什么在开始前检查装置气密性很重要？

2. What is the purpose of the saturated NaCl solution in the first wash bottle?

第一个洗气瓶中饱和食盐水的作用是什么？

Procedure | 操作步骤

1. Prepare collection bottles | 准备收集瓶

  • - Fill 3 gas collection bottles with water and invert them in a water trough
  • - 用水装满3个集气瓶并倒置在水槽中
  • - OR prepare dry bottles for upward displacement of air
  • - 或准备干燥的瓶子用于向上排空气法

2. Collect chlorine gas | 收集氯气

  • - Insert the gas delivery tube into the first collection bottle
  • - 将导气管插入第一个集气瓶
  • - Collect gas by downward displacement of water
  • - 用向下排水法收集气体

3. Test for fullness | 检验是否收集满

  • - Hold moist starch-KI test paper near the bottle mouth
  • - 将湿润的淀粉碘化钾试纸靠近瓶口
  • - When the paper turns blue-black, the bottle is full
  • - 当试纸变蓝黑色时，瓶子已满

4. Seal the bottles | 密封瓶子

  • - Cover each full bottle with a glass plate underwater
  • - 在水下用玻璃片盖住每个装满的瓶子
  • - Remove from water trough keeping the plate in place
  • - 保持玻璃片位置从水槽中取出

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Inquiry-Based Learning | 探究式学习
Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.
现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Preparation of Chlorine Gas | 氯气的制备

Chemical Reaction | 化学反应:


MnO₂ + 4HCl(concentrated) --Δ--> MnCl₂ + Cl₂↑ + 2H₂O

Reaction Conditions | 反应条件:

  • Concentrated HCl (37%) is required; dilute HCl will not react effectively
  • 需要浓盐酸（37%）；稀盐酸不能有效反应
  • Heating is necessary to increase the reaction rate
  • 需要加热以提高反应速率

Role of Reagents | 试剂作用:

  • MnO₂: Oxidizing agent (氧化剂)
  • HCl: Reducing agent and provides Cl⁻ ions (还原剂，提供Cl⁻离子)

2. Purification of Chlorine | 氯气的净化

Saturated NaCl Solution | 饱和食盐水:

  • Removes HCl gas (highly soluble in water)
  • 除去HCl气体（极易溶于水）
  • Chlorine has low solubility in saturated NaCl solution
  • 氯气在饱和食盐水中溶解度低

Concentrated H₂SO₄ | 浓硫酸:

  • Dries the chlorine gas by absorbing water vapor
  • 通过吸收水蒸气干燥氯气

3. Physical Properties | 物理性质

  • Color | 颜色: Yellow-green gas (黄绿色气体)
  • Odor | 气味: Pungent, irritating (刺激性气味)
  • Density | 密度: Denser than air (ρ = 3.21 g/L at STP) (比空气密度大)
  • Solubility | 溶解性: Moderately soluble in water (中等溶于水)
  • Toxicity | 毒性: Highly toxic (剧毒)

4. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Oxidizing Property - Reaction with Metals | 氧化性 - 与金属反应

Reaction with Copper | 与铜反应:


Cu + Cl₂ --Δ--> CuCl₂
  • Copper burns in chlorine with a brownish-yellow smoke
  • 铜在氯气中燃烧产生棕黄色烟
  • Product: Copper(II) chloride (CuCl₂)
  • 产物：氯化铜(II)

Reaction with Iron | 与铁反应:


2Fe + 3Cl₂ --Δ--> 2FeCl₃
  • Note: Forms Fe³⁺, not Fe²⁺, due to strong oxidizing power of Cl₂
  • 注意：由于Cl₂的强氧化性，生成Fe³⁺而非Fe²⁺

(2) Reaction with Water | 与水反应


Cl₂ + H₂O ⇌ HCl + HClO
  • This is a reversible reaction (可逆反应)
  • HClO (hypochlorous acid) is unstable and has strong oxidizing power
  • HClO（次氯酸）不稳定且具有强氧化性
  • HClO is the actual bleaching agent, not Cl₂
  • HClO是实际的漂白剂，而非Cl₂

Chlorine Water Composition | 氯水成分:

  • Molecules: Cl₂, H₂O, HClO
  • Ions: H⁺, Cl⁻, ClO⁻, OH⁻

(3) Bleaching Property | 漂白性质

Important Distinction | 重要区别:

  • Dry Cl₂ does NOT bleach (干燥的Cl₂不漂白)
  • Moist Cl₂ bleaches because it reacts with water to form HClO
  • 湿润的Cl₂漂白是因为与水反应生成HClO
  • HClO oxidizes colored compounds to colorless compounds
  • HClO将有色化合物氧化成无色化合物

Mechanism | 机理:


Cl₂ + H₂O → HCl + HClO
HClO → HCl + [O]
Colored compound + [O] → Colorless compound

Comparison with SO₂ Bleaching | 与SO₂漂白对比:

  • HClO bleaching: Oxidation, permanent (氧化漂白，永久性)
  • SO₂ bleaching: Combination, temporary (化合漂白，暂时性)

(4) Reaction with Alkali | 与碱反应

Reaction with NaOH | 与NaOH反应:


Cl₂ + 2NaOH → NaCl + NaClO + H₂O
  • This is a disproportionation reaction (歧化反应)
  • Chlorine is both oxidized (to ClO⁻) and reduced (to Cl⁻)
  • 氯既被氧化（成ClO⁻）又被还原（成Cl⁻）
  • Used industrially to produce bleach (工业上用于生产漂白液)
  • NaClO is the active ingredient in bleach (NaClO是漂白液的有效成分)

5. Uses of Chlorine | 氯气的用途

1. Water Disinfection | 水消毒

  • - Drinking water treatment (饮用水处理)
  • - Swimming pool sanitation (游泳池消毒)

2. Bleaching | 漂白

  • - Textile industry (纺织工业)
  • - Paper industry (造纸工业)

3. Chemical Production | 化工生产

  • - Hydrochloric acid: H₂ + Cl₂ → 2HCl
  • - Bleach: Cl₂ + 2NaOH → NaCl + NaClO + H₂O
  • - PVC plastic and other chlorinated compounds
  • - PVC塑料和其他氯化物

I. Laboratory Preparation of Chlorine | 氯气的实验室制法

Principle | 原理:

  • Oxidation (氧化) of concentrated HCl by MnO₂
  • MnO₂氧化浓HCl

Key Points | 要点:

1. Must use concentrated HCl, not dilute

必须使用浓盐酸，不能用稀盐酸

2. Heating is required

需要加热

3. Purification: saturated NaCl (remove HCl) → concentrated H₂SO₄ (dry)

净化：饱和食盐水（除HCl）→ 浓硫酸（干燥）

4. Collection: downward displacement of water or upward displacement of air

收集：向下排水法或向上排空气法

5. Tail gas must be absorbed by NaOH solution

尾气必须用NaOH溶液吸收

II. Properties of Chlorine | 氯气的性质

Physical Properties | 物理性质:

  • Yellow-green gas (黄绿色气体)
  • Pungent odor (刺激性气味)
  • Denser than air (比空气密度大)
  • Moderately soluble in water (中等溶于水)
  • Toxic (有毒)

Chemical Properties | 化学性质:

1. Strong oxidizing agent (强氧化剂)

2. Reacts with metals to form chlorides (与金属反应生成氯化物)

3. Reacts with water to form HCl and HClO (与水反应生成HCl和HClO)

4. Bleaching property (through HClO formation) (漂白性，通过生成HClO)

5. Reacts with alkali in disproportionation (与碱发生歧化反应)

III. Important Concepts | 重要概念

1. Bleaching Mechanism | 漂白机理

  • Cl₂ itself does NOT bleach (Cl₂本身不漂白)
  • Cl₂ + H₂O → HClO (actual bleaching agent)
  • HClO oxidizes colored compounds (HClO氧化有色物质)

2. Chlorine Water | 氯水

  • Fresh chlorine water: yellow-green, acidic, oxidizing, bleaching

新制氯水：黄绿色，酸性，氧化性，漂白性

  • Aged chlorine water: colorless (HClO decomposes), only acidic

久置氯水：无色（HClO分解），仅有酸性

3. Environmental Awareness | 环境意识

  • Chlorine is toxic and must not be released

氯气有毒，不可排放

  • Waste gas must be absorbed by alkali

废气必须用碱吸收
