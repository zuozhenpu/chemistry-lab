Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Inquiry-Based Learning | 探究式学习
Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.
现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Sodium Oxide (Na₂O) | 氧化钠

Preparation | 制备:


4Na + O₂ → 2Na₂O (at room temperature)

Properties | 性质:

  • White solid (白色固体)
  • Basic (碱性) oxide (碱性氧化物)
  • Reacts vigorously with water (与水剧烈反应)

Reaction with Water | 与水反应:


Na₂O + H₂O → 2NaOH

2. Sodium Peroxide (Na₂O₂) | 过氧化钠

Preparation | 制备:


2Na + O₂ --Δ--> Na₂O₂ (when heated)

Properties | 性质:

  • Pale yellow solid (淡黄色固体)
  • Strong oxidizing agent (强氧化剂)
  • Contains peroxide ion (O₂²⁻) (含有过氧根离子)

Important Reactions | 重要反应:

(1) With Water | 与水反应:


2Na₂O₂ + 2H₂O → 4NaOH + O₂↑
  • Produces oxygen gas (产生氧气)
  • Exothermic reaction (放热反应)

(2) With CO₂ | 与CO₂反应:


2Na₂O₂ + 2CO₂ → 2Na₂CO₃ + O₂↑
  • Absorbs CO₂ and releases O₂ (吸收CO₂并释放O₂)
  • Used in submarines and spacecraft (用于潜艇和航天器)

3. Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH) | 氢氧化钠

Common Names | 俗名: Caustic soda (苛性钠), Lye (碱液)

Properties | 性质:

  • White solid, highly soluble in water (白色固体，易溶于水)
  • Deliquescent (易潮解)
  • Dissolving in water is highly exothermic (溶于水强烈放热)

Chemical Reactions | 化学反应:

(1) With Acids | 与酸:


NaOH + HCl → NaCl + H₂O
2NaOH + H₂SO₄ → Na₂SO₄ + 2H₂O

(2) With Acidic Oxides | 与酸性氧化物:


2NaOH + CO₂ → Na₂CO₃ + H₂O
2NaOH + SO₂ → Na₂SO₃ + H₂O

(3) With Amphoteric Oxides | 与两性氧化物:


2NaOH + Al₂O₃ → 2NaAlO₂ + H₂O

4. Sodium Carbonate (Na₂CO₃) | 碳酸钠

Common Name | 俗名: Soda ash (纯碱)

Properties | 性质:

  • White powder, soluble in water (白色粉末，易溶于水)
  • Aqueous solution is alkaline (水溶液呈碱性)

Reactions | 反应:


Na₂CO₃ + 2HCl → 2NaCl + H₂O + CO₂↑

5. Sodium Bicarbonate (NaHCO₃) | 碳酸氢钠

Common Name | 俗名: Baking soda (小苏打)

Thermal Decomposition | 热分解:


2NaHCO₃ --Δ--> Na₂CO₃ + H₂O + CO₂↑

With Acids | 与酸:


NaHCO₃ + HCl → NaCl + H₂O + CO₂↑

6. Comparison: Na₂CO₃ vs. NaHCO₃ | 对比

Property Na₂CO₃ NaHCO₃
Solubility High Lower
Alkalinity Strong Weak
Thermal stability Stable Decomposes
With NaOH No reaction Forms Na₂CO₃
