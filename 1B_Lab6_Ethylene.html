Safety Guidelines | 安全指南

> ⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS | 重要安全警告

  • Concentrated Sulfuric Acid | 浓硫酸
- Extremely corrosive - wear goggles and gloves - 极具腐蚀性 - 佩戴护目镜和手套 - Add acid slowly along the wall of the flask - 沿烧瓶壁缓慢加入酸 - If spilled on skin: Rinse immediately with large amounts of water - 如溅到皮肤：立即用大量水冲洗
  • Temperature Control | 温度控制
- Must maintain 170°C - use thermometer! - 必须保持170°C - 使用温度计！ - Too low (140°C): produces ether instead - 过低（140°C）：产生乙醚 - Too high: carbonization occurs - 过高：发生碳化
  • Prevent Suck-back | 防止倒吸
- ALWAYS remove gas delivery tube from liquid BEFORE turning off heat - 关闭加热前务必先将导气管从液体中取出 - Never do it in reverse order! - 绝不可颠倒顺序！
  • Flammable Gas | 易燃气体
- Keep away from flames during collection - 收集时远离火焰 - Work in well-ventilated area - 在通风良好的区域工作

Part 1: Preparation of Ethylene | 乙烯的制备

Procedure | 操作步骤

  • Prepare the reaction mixture | 准备反应混合物
- Add 2.0 g of fine sand to the round-bottom flask - 向圆底烧瓶中加入2.0克细沙 - Add 10 mL of absolute ethanol - 加入10毫升无水乙醇 - Slowly add 5 mL of concentrated H₂SO₄ along the wall of the flask - 沿烧瓶壁缓慢加入5毫升浓硫酸 - Swirl gently to mix (CAUTION: mixture will heat up!) - 轻轻摇动混合（注意：混合物会发热！）
  • Set up apparatus | 组装装置
- Insert thermometer so bulb is in the liquid - 插入温度计使水银球在液体中 - Connect gas delivery tube - 连接导气管 - Pass gas through NaOH wash bottle first - 先通过NaOH洗气瓶
  • Heat and generate gas | 加热产生气体
- Heat the flask rapidly to 170°C - 快速加热烧瓶至170°C - Maintain temperature at 170°C (±5°C) - 保持温度在170°C（±5°C） - Wait 2-3 minutes for air to be purged - 等待2-3分钟排出空气
  • Collect ethylene | 收集乙烯
- Collect gas by downward displacement of water - 用向下排水法收集气体 - Fill 3 gas collection bottles - 装满3个集气瓶

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed observations, let's understand the principles.

现在你已经完成了观察，让我们来理解原理。

1. Preparation of Ethylene | 乙烯的制备

Chemical Reaction | 化学方程式: 
CH₃CH₂OH --concentrated H₂SO₄, 170°C--> CH₂=CH₂↑ + H₂O
Reaction Type | 反应类型: Elimination reaction (dehydration) 消去反应（脱水） Temperature Control | 温度控制:
  • 140°C: 2C₂H₅OH → C₂H₅OC₂H₅ + H₂O (ether formation)
  • 140°C: 2C₂H₅OH → C₂H₅OC₂H₅ + H₂O（生成乙醚）
  • 170°C: C₂H₅OH → CH₂=CH₂ + H₂O (ethylene formation)
  • 170°C: C₂H₅OH → CH₂=CH₂ + H₂O（生成乙烯）
  • >170°C: Carbonization (mixture turns black)
  • >170°C: 碳化（混合物变黑）
Role of Reagents | 试剂作用:
  • H₂SO₄: Catalyst and dehydrating agent (催化剂和脱水剂)
  • Sand: Prevents bumping and provides heat distribution (防止暴沸，提供均匀受热)

2. Molecular Structure | 分子结构

Molecular Formula | 分子式: C₂H₄ Key Features | 关键特征:
  • Contains C=C double bond (含有C=C双键)
  • Planar molecule - all atoms in same plane (平面分子 - 所有原子在同一平面)
  • Unsaturated hydrocarbon (不饱和烃)

3. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Addition Reaction with Bromine | 与溴的加成反应

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式: 
CH₂=CH₂ + Br₂ → CH₂BrCH₂Br
                (1,2-dibromoethane)
Observations Explained | 观察解释:
  • Reddish-brown bromine color disappears (红棕色溴消失)
  • Forms colorless 1,2-dibromoethane (生成无色1,2-二溴乙烷)
  • This is the characteristic test for alkenes (这是烯烃的特征测试)

(2) Oxidation by KMnO₄ | 被KMnO₄氧化

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式: 
3CH₂=CH₂ + 2KMnO₄ + 4H₂O → 3CH₂OHCH₂OH + 2MnO₂↓ + 2KOH
Observations Explained | 观察解释:
  • Purple KMnO₄ color fades (紫色KMnO₄褪色)
  • Brown MnO₂ precipitate may form (可能形成棕色MnO₂沉淀)
  • Ethylene is oxidized (乙烯被氧化)

(3) Combustion | 燃烧

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式: 
C₂H₄ + 3O₂ → 2CO₂ + 2H₂O
Observations Explained | 观察解释:
  • Bright, luminous flame with black smoke (明亮的火焰伴有黑烟)
  • Black smoke = incomplete combustion due to high C:H ratio
  • 黑烟 = 由于高C:H比导致的不完全燃烧
  • Water vapor condenses on cold surface (水蒸气在冷表面凝结)
  • CO₂ turns limewater milky (CO₂使石灰水变浑浊)

4. Comparison: Ethylene vs. Ethane | 对比：乙烯与乙烷

PropertyEthylene (C₂H₄)Ethane (C₂H₆)
StructureC=C double bondC-C single bond
TypeUnsaturatedSaturated
Main reactionAdditionSubstitution
With Br₂DecolorizesNo reaction
With KMnO₄DecolorizesNo reaction
CombustionBright flame, smokePale blue, no smoke

Knowledge Summary | 知识点梳理

I. Laboratory Preparation | 实验室制法

Principle | 原理: Dehydration of ethanol 乙醇脱水 Critical Conditions | 关键条件:
  • Temperature: 170°C (exactly!)
  • 温度：170°C（精确！）
  • Catalyst (催化剂): Concentrated H₂SO₄
  • 催化剂：浓硫酸
Purification | 净化:
  • NaOH solution removes acidic impurities
  • NaOH溶液除去酸性杂质

II. Properties | 性质

Physical | 物理:
  • Colorless gas (无色气体)
  • Slightly sweet odor (略带甜味)
  • Slightly less dense than air (比空气略轻)
  • Sparingly soluble (微溶) in water (微溶于水)
Chemical | 化学:
  • Addition reactions (characteristic)
  • 加成反应（特征）
  • Oxidation (氧化) reactions
  • 氧化反应
  • Combustion
  • 燃烧

III. Important Concepts | 重要概念

Addition vs. Substitution | 加成与取代:
  • Addition: Unsaturated compounds add atoms across double bond
  • 加成：不饱和化合物在双键两端加成原子
  • Substitution: Saturated compounds replace atoms
  • 取代：饱和化合物替换原子
Testing for Alkenes | 烯烃的检验:
  • Bromine water decolorization (溴水褪色)
  • Acidified KMnO₄ decolorization (酸性KMnO₄褪色)
