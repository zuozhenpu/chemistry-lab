Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Molecular Structure of Ethylene | 乙烯的分子结构

Molecular Formula | 分子式: C₂H₄ or CH₂=CH₂

Functional Group | 官能团: Carbon-carbon double bond (C=C) | 碳碳双键

Key Structural Features | 关键结构特征:

  • Contains a carbon-carbon double bond
  • 含有碳碳双键
  • Planar molecule with 120° bond angles
  • 平面分子，键角120°
  • Electrons in the π bond are easily accessible for reactions
  • π键中的电子容易参与反应

2. Physical Properties | 物理性质

Appearance | 外观:

  • Colorless gas at room temperature
  • 室温下为无色气体
  • Slightly sweet odor
  • 略带甜味

Density | 密度:

  • 1.178 g/L (slightly denser than air)
  • 1.178克/升（比空气略重）

Boiling Point | 沸点:

  • -103.7°C (very low, gas at room temperature)
  • -103.7°C（非常低，室温下为气体）

Solubility | 溶解性:

  • Slightly soluble in water
  • 微溶于水
  • More soluble in organic solvents
  • 更易溶于有机溶剂

3. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Addition Reactions | 加成反应

With Bromine | 与溴反应:


CH₂=CH₂ + Br₂ → CH₂Br-CH₂Br
                (1,2-dibromoethane)

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Red-brown bromine color disappears (decolorization)
  • 红棕色溴颜色消失（脱色）
  • Forms colorless 1,2-dibromoethane
  • 形成无色的1,2-二溴乙烷
  • Reaction is rapid at room temperature
  • 室温下反应迅速

With KMnO₄ | 与高锰酸钾反应:


CH₂=CH₂ + [O] + H₂O → CH₂(OH)-CH₂(OH)
            (ethane-1,2-diol)

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Purple KMnO₄ color disappears (reduced to Mn²⁺)
  • 紫色高锰酸钾消失（还原为Mn²⁺）
  • Forms ethylene glycol (ethane-1,2-diol)
  • 形成乙二醇
  • Solution becomes basic (phenolphthalein turns pink)
  • 溶液变碱性（酚酞变粉红色）

(2) Combustion | 燃烧

Complete Combustion | 完全燃烧:


CH₂=CH₂ + 3O₂ → 2CO₂ + 2H₂O

Characteristics | 特点:

  • Produces a luminous, sooty flame
  • 产生发光、有烟的火焰
  • Incomplete combustion produces carbon (soot)
  • 不完全燃烧产生碳（烟灰）
  • High heat of combustion
  • 燃烧热高

(3) Polymerization | 聚合

Polymerization Reaction | 聚合反应:


n CH₂=CH₂ → (-CH₂-CH₂-)n
              (polyethylene)

Conditions | 条件:

  • High pressure (1000-3000 atm)
  • 高压（1000-3000大气压）
  • High temperature (150-300°C)
  • 高温（150-300°C）
  • Catalyst (Ziegler-Natta or free radical initiator)
  • 催化剂（齐格勒-纳塔或自由基引发剂）

4. Industrial Production of Ethylene | 乙烯的工业生产

(a) Steam Cracking | 蒸汽裂解

Process | 过程:

  • Heat petroleum fractions (naphtha, ethane, propane) to 800-900°C
  • 将石油馏分（石脑油、乙烷、丙烷）加热到800-900°C
  • C-C bonds break to form smaller molecules including ethylene
  • C-C键断裂形成包括乙烯在内的小分子

Reaction Example | 反应示例:


C₈H₁₈ (octane) → CH₂=CH₂ + other products

(b) Ethanol Dehydration | 乙醇脱水

Reaction | 反应:


CH₃CH₂OH --H₂SO₄, 170°C--> CH₂=CH₂ + H₂O

5. Uses of Ethylene | 乙烯的用途

1. Polyethylene Production | 聚乙烯生产

  • - Most important use (over 50% of production)
  • - 最重要的用途（占产量50%以上）
  • - HDPE (high-density) and LDPE (low-density)
