Safety Guidelines | 安全指南

⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS | 重要安全警告

1. Visual Safety | 视觉安全

  • - Do not look directly at burning magnesium. It emits UV light that can damage eyes.
  • - 不要直视燃烧的镁。它发射出的紫外线会伤害眼睛。
  • - Use cobalt glass or look indirectly if possible.
  • - 尽可能使用钴玻璃或间接观察。

2. Chemical Safety | 化学品安全

  • - NaNO₂ is toxic if swallowed. Wash hands thoroughly.
  • - 吞食NaNO₂有毒。彻底洗手。

3. Apparatus Safety | 装置安全

  • - Prevent suck-back: Remove delivery tube from water before stopping heat.
  • - 防止倒吸：停止加热前将导管从水中取出。

Part 2: Chemical Properties - Reaction with Magnesium | 化学性质 - 与镁反应

Materials and Equipment | 材料与仪器

Chemicals | 化学药品:

  • Sodium nitrite (NaNO₂) powder | 亚硝酸钠粉末
  • Ammonium chloride (NH₄Cl) powder | 氯化铵粉末
  • Magnesium ribbon (Mg) | 镁条
  • Distilled water | 蒸馏水

Equipment | 实验仪器:

  • Round-bottom flask or large test tube | 圆底烧瓶或大试管
  • Delivery tube and rubber stopper | 导管和橡胶塞
  • Alcohol lamp | 酒精灯
  • Gas collection bottles (Gas jars) | 集气瓶
  • Water trough | 水槽
  • Crucible tongs | 坩埚钳
  • Sandpaper | 砂纸
  • Matches/Lighter | 火柴/打火机

Procedure | 操作步骤

Step 1: Preparation of Mg | 准备镁条

  • Polish a strip of magnesium ribbon with sandpaper to remove oxide layer.
  • 用砂纸打磨镁条以去除氧化层。

Step 2: Combustion | 燃烧

  • Hold the magnesium ribbon with crucible tongs.
  • 用坩埚钳夹住镁条。
  • Ignite it using the alcohol lamp (outside the jar).
  • 用酒精灯点燃它（在集气瓶外）。
  • Immediately insert the burning magnesium into the jar of Nitrogen.
  • 立即将燃烧的镁条插入氮气瓶中。

  • Observe if combustion continues.

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Physical Properties | 氮的物理性质

Appearance | 外观:

  • Colorless, odorless gas (无色无味气体)
  • Tasteless (无味)

Density | 密度:

  • Slightly less dense than air (比空气略轻)
  • 1.25 g/L at STP (标准状况下1.25克/升)

Boiling Point | 沸点:

  • -196°C (very low) (很低)
  • Used as cryogenic liquid (用作低温液体)

Solubility | 溶解性:

  • Sparingly soluble (微溶) in water (微溶于水)

2. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

Stability | 稳定性:

  • N≡N triple bond is very strong (N≡N三键很强)
  • Bond energy: 946 kJ/mol (键能：946 kJ/mol)
  • Chemically inert at room temperature (室温下化学性质不活泼)

(1) Reaction with Hydrogen | 与氢气反应

Haber Process | 哈伯法:


N₂ + 3H₂ ⇌ 2NH₃ (reversible, exothermic)
  • Requires high temperature (450-500°C) (需要高温)
  • High pressure (200-300 atm) (高压)
  • Iron catalyst (铁催化剂)
  • Industrial production of ammonia (工业生产氨)

(2) Reaction with Oxygen | 与氧气反应

At High Temperature | 高温下:


N₂ + O₂ --高温--> 2NO (nitrogen monoxide)
  • Occurs in lightning (闪电中发生)
  • Occurs in car engines (汽车发动机中发生)
  • Endothermic reaction (吸热反应)

Further Oxidation | 进一步氧化:


2NO + O₂ → 2NO₂ (nitrogen dioxide)

(3) Reaction with Metals | 与金属反应

With Magnesium | 与镁:


3Mg + N₂ --Δ--> Mg₃N₂ (magnesium nitride)
  • Requires heating (需要加热)
  • Yellow-green solid (黄绿色固体)

With Lithium | 与锂:


6Li + N₂ → 2Li₃N (lithium nitride)
  • Can occur at room temperature (室温下可发生)

3. Nitrogen Oxides | 氮的氧化物

Nitric Oxide (NO) | 一氧化氮:

  • Colorless gas (无色气体)
  • Slightly soluble in water (微溶于水)
  • Oxidized rapidly in air (在空气中迅速被氧化)

Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂) | 二氧化氮:

  • Red-brown gas (红棕色气体)
  • Pungent odor (刺激性气味)
  • Toxic (有毒)

Dinitrogen Tetroxide (N₂O₄) | 四氧化二氮:


2NO₂ ⇌ N₂O₄ (equilibrium, colorless)
  • Exists in equilibrium with NO₂ (与NO₂平衡存在)

Dinitrogen Oxide (N₂O) | 一氧化二氮:

  • Colorless gas (无色气体)
  • Sweet odor (甜味)
  • Laughing gas (笑气)
  • Anesthetic (麻醉剂)

4. Nitrogen Cycle | 氮循环

Nitrogen Fixation | 固氮:

1. Lightning (闪电)

2. Nitrogen-fixing bacteria (固氮细菌)

3. Industrial Haber process (工业哈伯法)

Nitrification | 硝化:


NH₃ → NO₂⁻ → NO₃⁻

Denitrification | 反硝化:


NO₃⁻ → NO₂⁻ → N₂

5. Uses of Nitrogen | 氮的用途

1. Inert Atmosphere | 惰性气氛:

  • Food packaging (食品包装)
  • Prevents oxidation (防止氧化)
  • Electronics manufacturing (电子制造)

2. Cryogenic Applications | 低温应用:

  • Liquid nitrogen for freezing (液氮冷冻)
  • Medical procedures (医疗程序)
  • Food preservation (食品保存)

3. Chemical Production | 化工生产:

  • Ammonia synthesis (合成氨)
  • Nitric acid production (生产硝酸)
  • Fertilizers (肥料)

4. Tire Inflation | 轮胎充气:

  • More stable than air (比空气更稳定)
  • Less pressure variation (压力变化小)

6. Environmental Concerns | 环境问题

NOₓ Pollution | 氮氧化物污染:

  • From vehicle emissions (来自车辆排放)
  • From power plants (来自发电厂)
  • Contributes to acid rain (导致酸雨)
  • Forms photochemical smog (形成光化学烟雾)

Control Measures | 控制措施:

  • Catalytic converters (催化转化器)
  • Selective catalytic reduction (选择性催化还原)
  • Low-NOₓ burners (低氮氧化物燃烧器)
