Safety Guidelines | 安全指南

⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS | 重要安全警告

1. Fire Hazard | 火灾危险

  • - Highly Flammable. Keep away from open flames.
  • - 高度易燃。远离明火。
  • - In case of fire: Cover with wet cloth or sand. Do NOT pour water.
  • - 万一着火：用湿布或沙子覆盖。不要倒水。

2. Sodium Safety | 钠安全

  • - No Water Contact. Ensure all apparatus contacting sodium is dry.
  • - 无水接触。确保所有接触钠的仪器干燥。
  • - Dispose Properly. React waste sodium with ethanol, not water.
  • - 正确处理。让废钠与乙醇反应，而不是水。

3. Corrosive Acid | 腐蚀性酸

  • - Conc. H₂SO₄. Handle with gloves. Add acid to alcohol slowly with cooling/stirring.
  • - 浓H₂SO₄。戴手套处理。在冷却/搅拌下将酸缓慢加入酒精。

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Molecular Structure of Ethanol | 乙醇的分子结构

Molecular Formula | 分子式: C₂H₅OH or CH₃CH₂OH

Functional Group | 官能团: Hydroxyl group (-OH) | 羟基

Key Structural Features | 关键结构特征:

  • Ethanol is NOT an ionic compound (not C₂H₅⁺ and OH⁻)
  • 乙醇不是离子化合物（不是C₂H₅⁺和OH⁻）
  • It is a covalent compound with a polar O-H bond
  • 它是具有极性O-H键的共价化合物
  • The hydroxyl hydrogen can be replaced by active metals
  • 羟基氢可以被活泼金属置换

2. Physical Properties | 物理性质

Appearance | 外观:

  • Colorless, transparent liquid
  • 无色透明液体

Odor | 气味:

  • Characteristic pleasant odor
  • 特征性的宜人气味

Density | 密度:

  • 0.789 g/cm³ (less dense than water)
  • 0.789克/立方厘米（比水密度小）

Boiling Point | 沸点:

  • 78.5°C (lower than water due to weaker hydrogen bonding)
  • 78.5°C（由于氢键较弱，低于水）

Solubility | 溶解性:

  • Miscible with water in all proportions
  • 与水以任意比例互溶
  • Good solvent for many organic and inorganic substances
  • 许多有机和无机物质的良好溶剂

Volatility | 挥发性:

  • Easily evaporates at room temperature
  • 室温下易挥发

3. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Reaction with Sodium (Substitution) | 与钠反应（取代反应）

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


2C₂H₅OH + 2Na → 2C₂H₅ONa + H₂↑

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Sodium sinks because ethanol density (0.789 g/cm³) > sodium density (0.97 g/cm³)
  • 钠下沉是因为乙醇密度（0.789克/立方厘米）>钠密度（0.97克/立方厘米）
  • Reaction is less vigorous than Na + H₂O because the O-H bond in ethanol is stronger
  • 反应比Na + H₂O缓和，因为乙醇中的O-H键更强
  • Hydrogen gas is produced (burns with a pale blue flame)
  • 产生氢气（燃烧时呈淡蓝色火焰）

Comparison with Water | 与水对比:

Property Na + H₂O Na + C₂H₅OH
Reaction rate Vigorous Moderate
Sodium position Floats Sinks
Sodium state Melts into ball Doesn't melt
Product NaOH + H₂ C₂H₅ONa + H₂

Activity Order | 活性顺序:

H in H₂O > H in -OH of ethanol > H in -CH₃ of ethanol

(2) Oxidation Reactions | 氧化反应

(a) Combustion (Complete Oxidation) | 燃烧（完全氧化)

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


C₂H₅OH + 3O₂ → 2CO₂ + 3H₂O

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Pale blue flame (characteristic of alcohol combustion)
  • 淡蓝色火焰（醇燃烧的特征）
  • Releases large amount of heat (exothermic)
  • 释放大量热（放热）
  • Water vapor condenses on cold surface
  • 水蒸气在冷表面上冷凝
  • CO₂ turns limewater milky
  • CO₂使石灰水变浑浊

(b) Catalytic Oxidation (Partial Oxidation) | 催化氧化（部分氧化)

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


2C₂H₅OH + O₂ --Cu or Ag, Δ--> 2CH₃CHO + 2H₂O

Mechanism | 机理:


Step 1: 2Cu + O₂ → 2CuO (copper turns black)
Step 2: CuO + C₂H₅OH → Cu + CH₃CHO + H₂O (black turns red)

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Copper wire turns black (CuO formation) when heated in air
  • 铜丝在空气中加热时变黑（形成CuO）
  • Black color disappears (CuO reduced to Cu) when inserted in ethanol
  • 插入乙醇时黑色消失（CuO被还原成Cu）
  • Pungent odor (刺激性气味) indicates formation of acetaldehyde (CH₃CHO)
  • 刺激性气味表明生成了乙醛（CH₃CHO）
  • Copper acts as a catalyst (regenerated in the process)
  • 铜作为催化剂（在过程中再生）

(3) Esterification (Substitution) | 酯化反应（取代反应）

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


CH₃COOH + C₂H₅OH ⇌ CH₃COOC₂H₅ + H₂O
                    (ethyl acetate)

Reaction Conditions | 反应条件:

  • Catalyst (催化剂): Concentrated H₂SO₄ (催化剂：浓硫酸)
  • Temperature: 70-80°C (water bath) (温度：70-80°C（水浴））

Role of Reagents | 试剂作用:

  • Concentrated H₂SO₄: Catalyst and dehydrating agent
  • 浓硫酸：催化剂和脱水剂
  • Saturated Na₂CO₃: Neutralizes excess acid, dissolves ethanol, decreases ester solubility
  • 饱和Na₂CO₃：中和过量的酸，溶解乙醇，降低酯的溶解度

Mechanism | 机理:

  • "Acid loses -OH, alcohol loses -H"
  • "酸脱羟基，醇脱氢"
  • The oxygen in the ester comes from the alcohol, not the acid
  • 酯中的氧来自醇，而非酸

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Fruity odor indicates ester formation (ethyl acetate)
  • 果香味表明酯的生成（乙酸乙酯）
  • Oily layer on top (ester is less dense than water and immiscible)
  • 顶部油层（酯比水密度小且不互溶）
  • Bubbles when added to Na₂CO₃ (CO₂ from neutralization of excess acid)
  • 加入Na₂CO₃时产生气泡（中和过量酸产生的CO₂）

4. Industrial Production of Ethanol | 乙醇的工业生产

(a) Fermentation Method (Traditional) | 发酵法（传统）

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


C₆H₁₂O₆ --enzymes--> 2C₂H₅OH + 2CO₂↑

Advantages | 优点:

  • Uses renewable resources (grains, fruits)
  • 使用可再生资源（谷物、水果）
  • Simple process
  • 工艺简单

Disadvantages | 缺点:

  • Consumes food resources
  • 消耗粮食资源
  • Low ethanol concentration (≤15%)
  • 乙醇浓度低（≤15%）
  • Requires distillation for purification
  • 需要蒸馏纯化

(b) Ethylene Hydration (Modern Industrial) | 乙烯水化法（现代工业）

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


CH₂=CH₂ + H₂O --catalyst, heat, pressure--> CH₃CH₂OH

Conditions | 条件:

  • Catalyst (催化剂): Phosphoric acid or silica (催化剂：磷酸或硅酸)
  • Temperature: ~300°C (温度：约300°C)
  • Pressure: 6-7 MPa (压力：6-7兆帕)

Advantages | 优点:

  • Uses petroleum resources (doesn't consume food)
  • 使用石油资源（不消耗粮食）
  • High efficiency and concentration
  • 高效率和浓度
  • Large-scale production
  • 大规模生产

  • 官能团：-OH（羟基）
  • Covalent compound, not ionic
  • 共价化合物，非离子化合物

Physical Properties | 物理性质:

  • Colorless liquid, pleasant odor
  • 无色液体，宜人气味
  • Miscible with water
  • 与水互溶
  • Volatile, flammable
  • 易挥发，易燃
  • Good solvent
  • 良好溶剂

II. Chemical Reactions | 化学反应

1. Substitution with Active Metals | 与活泼金属的取代反应

  • Replaces H in -OH group
  • 置换-OH基团中的H
  • Less reactive than water
  • 比水反应性低

2. Oxidation | 氧化

  • Combustion → CO₂ + H₂O
  • 燃烧→CO₂ + H₂O
  • Catalytic oxidation → CH₃CHO (acetaldehyde)
  • 催化氧化→CH₃CHO（乙醛）

  • With carboxylic acids → esters
  • 与羧酸→酯
  • Requires acid catalyst and heat
  • 需要酸催化剂和加热

III. Important Concepts | 重要概念

75% Ethanol for Disinfection | 75%乙醇消毒

  • Most effective concentration
  • 最有效浓度
  • Higher concentrations cause rapid protein coagulation (protective layer)
  • 更高浓度导致蛋白质快速凝固（保护层）
  • Lower concentrations insufficient for denaturation
  • 更低浓度不足以使蛋白质变性

Ethanol vs. Water Reactivity | 乙醇与水的反应性

  • H₂O more reactive with Na than C₂H₅OH
  • H₂O比C₂H₅OH与Na反应性更强
  • Reason: Electron-donating effect of C₂H₅- group strengthens O-H bond
  • 原因：C₂H₅-基团的推电子效应增强了O-H键
