Safety Guidelines | 安全指南

⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY WARNINGS | 重要安全警告

1. Nitrogen Oxides | 氮氧化物

  • - NO₂ is highly toxic. All reactions producing NO/NO₂ MUST be done in a fume hood.
  • - NO₂剧毒。所有产生NO/NO₂的反应必须在通风橱中进行。

2. Concentrated Nitric Acid | 浓硝酸

  • - Highly corrosive and oxidizing. Gloves and goggles are mandatory.
  • - 强腐蚀性和氧化性。必须佩戴手套和护目镜。

3. Ammonia | 氨气

  • - Irritating to respiratory tract. Ensure good ventilation.
  • - 对呼吸道有刺激性。确保良好通风。

Note: This part demonstrates the difference between reaction with Dilute vs Concentrated acid.

Procedure | 操作步骤

Step 1: Preparation of Copper | 准备铜丝

  • Take two pieces of copper wire.
  • 取两段铜丝。
  • Coil one end into a spiral shape.
  • 将一端卷成螺旋状。
  • Prepare two rubber stoppers with a small notch cut on the side (to hold the wire).
  • 准备两个橡胶塞，侧面切一个小凹槽（用于卡住铜丝）。

Step 2: Reaction Setup | 反应设置

  • Tube A: Add small amount of Concentrated Nitric Acid.
  • 试管A：加入少量浓硝酸。
  • Tube B: Add small amount of Dilute Nitric Acid.
  • 试管B：加入少量稀硝酸。
  • Use the stopper to hold the copper wire so it can be lowered into the acid or pulled up.
  • 用橡胶塞固定铜丝，使其可以放入酸中或拉起。
  • ⚠️ Perform in Fume Hood!
  • ⚠️ 在通风橱中进行！

Step 3: Reaction Observation | 反应观察

  • Lower the copper wire into the acids simultaneously (or one by one).
  • 将铜丝同时（或逐一）放入酸中。
  • Observe the gas color in the solution and in the tube.
  • 观察溶液中和试管中气体的颜色。

Step 4: NO interaction with Air | NO与空气的相互作用

  • For the Dilute acid tube (Tube B), once reaction starts and gas is generated, remove the stopper briefly.
  • 对于稀酸试管（试管B），反应开始产生气体后，短暂取下橡胶塞。
  • Observe gas color change at the mouth of the tube.

Part 2: The Ammonia Fountain | 氨气喷泉

Materials and Equipment | 材料与仪器

Chemicals | 化学药品:

  • Copper wire (spiraled) | 铜丝（螺旋状）
  • Dilute Nitric Acid (HNO₃) | 稀硝酸

  • Ammonia gas (prepared or source) | 氨气（制备或气源）
  • Phenolphthalein solution | 酚酞溶液
  • Water | 水

Equipment | 实验仪器:

  • Test tubes with rubber stoppers (notched) | 带橡胶塞的试管（有凹槽）
  • Round-bottom flask (dry) | 圆底烧瓶（干燥）
  • Rubber stopper with glass tube and dropper | 带玻璃管和胶头滴管的橡胶塞
  • Beaker | 烧杯
  • Iron stand with clamp | 铁架台和铁夹

Procedure | 操作步骤

Step 1: Ammonia Collection | 氨气收集

  • Collect dry Ammonia gas in a round-bottom flask (using method from pre-lab).
  • 在圆底烧瓶中收集干燥氨气（使用预习中的方法）。
  • Stopper tightly with a solid stopper until ready.
  • 用实心塞塞紧直到准备好。

Step 2: Fountain Setup | 喷泉设置

  • Material Checklist: Round-bottom flask with NH₃, Rubber stopper equipped with a long glass tube and a medicine dropper (filled with water).
  • 材料清单：装有NH₃的圆底烧瓶，配有长玻璃管和胶头滴管（装满水）的橡胶塞。
  • Beaker filled with water + Phenolphthalein indicator (Wait... water should be pink? No, Phenolphthalein is clear in neutral, pink in base). Add Phenolphthalein to the water in the beaker.
  • 烧杯装水+酚酞指示剂。（向烧杯中的水加入酚酞）。
  • Invert the flask on an iron stand. Submerge the long glass tube into the beaker.
  • 将烧瓶倒置在铁架台上。将长玻璃管浸入烧杯中。

Step 3: Initiation | 启动

  • Squeeze the rubber dropper to inject a small amount of water into the flask.
  • 挤压胶头滴管，向烧瓶中注入少量水。
  • Open the clamp (if any) on the long tube.
  • 打开长管上的夹子（如果有）。
  • Observe the "Fountain".
  • 观察“喷泉”。

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Ammonia (NH₃) | 氨

Properties | 性质:

  • Colorless gas (无色气体)
  • Pungent, characteristic odor (刺激性特征气味)
  • Less dense than air (比空气密度小)
  • Highly soluble (易溶) in water (极易溶于水)

Aqueous Solution | 水溶液:


NH₃ + H₂O ⇌ NH₃·H₂O ⇌ NH₄⁺ + OH⁻
  • Weak base (弱碱)
  • Turns red litmus blue (使红色石蕊变蓝)
  • pH > 7

Reactions as a Base | 作为碱的反应:


NH₃ + HCl → NH₄Cl (white smoke)
2NH₃ + H₂SO₄ → (NH₄)₂SO₄
NH₃ + HNO₃ → NH₄NO₃

Reducing Properties | 还原性:


2NH₃ + 3CuO --Δ--> 3Cu + N₂ + 3H₂O
4NH₃ + 3O₂ --Δ--> 2N₂ + 6H₂O
4NH₃ + 5O₂ --催化剂,Δ--> 4NO + 6H₂O (industrial)

Complex Formation | 配合物形成:


Cu²⁺ + 4NH₃ → [Cu(NH₃)₄]²⁺ (deep blue)
Ag⁺ + 2NH₃ → [Ag(NH₃)₂]⁺ (colorless)

2. Ammonium Salts | 铵盐

Ammonium Chloride (NH₄Cl) | 氯化铵:

Thermal Decomposition | 热分解:


NH₄Cl --Δ--> NH₃↑ + HCl↑ (reversible)

Hydrolysis | 水解:


NH₄⁺ + H₂O ⇌ NH₃·H₂O + H⁺
  • Solution is acidic (溶液呈酸性)

Ammonium Nitrate (NH₄NO₃) | 硝酸铵:


NH₄NO₃ --Δ--> N₂O↑ + 2H₂O
  • Explosive (爆炸性) when heated strongly (强热时爆炸)
  • Used as fertilizer (用作肥料)

Ammonium Sulfate [(NH₄)₂SO₄] | 硫酸铵:

  • Important nitrogen fertilizer (重要氮肥)
  • Non-explosive (不爆炸)
  • Acidic (酸性) in solution (溶液呈酸性)

Detection of NH₄⁺ | NH₄⁺的检验:


NH₄⁺ + OH⁻ --Δ--> NH₃↑ + H₂O
  • Add NaOH and heat (加NaOH并加热)
  • Ammonia gas turns moist red litmus blue (氨气使湿润的红色石蕊变蓝)

3. Nitric Acid (HNO₃) | 硝酸

Properties | 性质:

  • Colorless liquid when pure (纯净时为无色液体)
  • Yellow-brown when impure (含杂质时呈黄棕色，含NO₂)
  • Strong acid (强酸)
  • Strong oxidizing agent (强氧化剂)

Acidic Properties | 酸性:


HNO₃ + NaOH → NaNO₃ + H₂O
2HNO₃ + CuO → Cu(NO₃)₂ + H₂O

Oxidizing Properties | 氧化性:

With Metals | 与金属:

Dilute HNO₃:


3Cu + 8HNO₃(dilute) → 3Cu(NO₃)₂ + 2NO↑ + 4H₂O

Concentrated HNO₃:


Cu + 4HNO₃(conc) → Cu(NO₃)₂ + 2NO₂↑ + 2H₂O

Passivation | 钝化:

  • Fe, Al passivated by concentrated HNO₃ (cold) (Fe、Al被浓硝酸（冷）钝化)
  • Forms protective oxide layer (形成保护性氧化层)

With Non-metals | 与非金属:


C + 4HNO₃(conc) --Δ--> CO₂↑ + 4NO₂↑ + 2H₂O
S + 6HNO₃(conc) --Δ--> H₂SO₄ + 6NO₂↑ + 2H₂O

Aqua Regia | 王水:


HNO₃ + 3HCl → NOCl + Cl₂ + 2H₂O
  • Mixture of HNO₃ and HCl (1:3) (HNO₃和HCl的混合物（1:3）)
  • Can dissolve gold and platinum (能溶解金和铂)

4. Industrial Production of HNO₃ | 硝酸的工业生产

Ostwald Process | 奥斯特瓦尔德法:

Step 1: Oxidation of ammonia | 氨的氧化:


4NH₃ + 5O₂ --Pt催化剂,Δ--> 4NO + 6H₂O

Step 2: Oxidation of NO | NO的氧化:


2NO + O₂ → 2NO₂

Step 3: Absorption in water | 水吸收:


4NO₂ + O₂ + 2H₂O → 4HNO₃

5. Nitrates | 硝酸盐

Thermal Decomposition | 热分解:

Nitrates of active metals | 活泼金属硝酸盐:


2NaNO₃ --Δ--> 2NaNO₂ + O₂↑

Nitrates of less active metals | 不活泼金属硝酸盐:


2Cu(NO₃)₂ --Δ--> 2CuO + 4NO₂↑ + O₂↑

Nitrates of very inactive metals | 很不活泼金属硝酸盐:


2AgNO₃ --Δ--> 2Ag + 2NO₂↑ + O₂↑

6. Applications | 应用

NH₃:

  • Fertilizer production (肥料生产)
  • Refrigerant (制冷剂)
  • Cleaning agent (清洁剂)
  • Chemical synthesis (化学合成)

HNO₃:

  • Fertilizer production (肥料生产)
  • Explosive (爆炸性)s (TNT, nitroglycerin) (炸药（TNT、硝化甘油）)
  • Metal etching (金属蚀刻)
  • Rocket propellant (火箭推进剂)

Ammonium Salts | 铵盐:

  • Fertilizers (肥料)
  • Explosive (爆炸性)s (炸药)
  • Food additives (食品添加剂)
