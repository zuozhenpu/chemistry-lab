Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Aluminum Hydroxide [Al(OH)₃] | 氢氧化铝

Preparation | 制备:


AlCl₃ + 3NaOH → Al(OH)₃↓ + 3NaCl
Al₂(SO₄)₃ + 6NaOH → 2Al(OH)₃↓ + 3Na₂SO₄

Properties | 性质:

  • White gelatinous precipitate (白色胶状沉淀)
  • Amphoteric (两性) hydroxide (两性氢氧化物)
  • Insoluble (不溶) in water (不溶于水)

Amphoteric Reactions | 两性反应:

With Acids | 与酸:


Al(OH)₃ + 3HCl → AlCl₃ + 3H₂O
2Al(OH)₃ + 3H₂SO₄ → Al₂(SO₄)₃ + 6H₂O

With Bases | 与碱:


Al(OH)₃ + NaOH → NaAlO₂ + 2H₂O
Al(OH)₃ + NaOH → Na[Al(OH)₄] (excess NaOH)

Thermal Decomposition | 热分解:


2Al(OH)₃ --Δ--> Al₂O₃ + 3H₂O

2. Sodium Aluminate (NaAlO₂) | 偏铝酸钠

Preparation | 制备:


2Al + 2NaOH + 2H₂O → 2NaAlO₂ + 3H₂↑
Al₂O₃ + 2NaOH → 2NaAlO₂ + H₂O (molten)
Al(OH)₃ + NaOH → NaAlO₂ + 2H₂O

Hydrolysis | 水解:


NaAlO₂ + 2H₂O ⇌ NaOH + Al(OH)₃
  • Solution is alkaline (溶液呈碱性)

Reaction with CO₂ | 与CO₂反应:


NaAlO₂ + CO₂ + 2H₂O → Al(OH)₃↓ + NaHCO₃

3. Aluminum Sulfate [Al₂(SO₄)₃] | 硫酸铝

Preparation | 制备:


2Al(OH)₃ + 3H₂SO₄ → Al₂(SO₄)₃ + 6H₂O
Al₂O₃ + 3H₂SO₄ → Al₂(SO₄)₃ + 3H₂O

Hydrolysis | 水解:


Al³⁺ + 3H₂O ⇌ Al(OH)₃ + 3H⁺
  • Aqueous solution is acidic (水溶液呈酸性)

Applications | 应用:

  • Water purification (净水)
  • Paper manufacturing (造纸)
  • Dyeing mordant (染色媒染剂)

4. Alum [KAl(SO₄)₂·12H₂O] | 明矾

Common Name | 俗名: Potassium alum (钾明矾)

Properties | 性质:

  • Colorless crystals (无色晶体)
  • Soluble (可溶) in water (易溶于水)
  • Astringent taste (涩味)

Hydrolysis | 水解:


Al³⁺ + 3H₂O ⇌ Al(OH)₃(colloid) + 3H⁺
  • Forms colloidal Al(OH)₃ (形成胶体Al(OH)₃)
  • Adsorbs impurities (吸附杂质)

Applications | 应用:

  • Water purification (净水)
  • Food additive (食品添加剂)
  • Medical astringent (医用收敛剂)

5. Important Reaction Sequences | 重要反应序列

Aluminum → Aluminate → Hydroxide → Oxide:


Al --NaOH--> NaAlO₂ --CO₂--> Al(OH)₃ --Δ--> Al₂O₃

Interconversion of Aluminum Compounds | 铝化合物的相互转化:


        +HCl           +NaOH(excess)
Al₂O₃ -------> AlCl₃ ----------------> NaAlO₂
   ↑              ↓                        ↓
   |         +NaOH(limited)            +CO₂
   |              ↓                        ↓
   +Δ         Al(OH)₃ <-------------------+

6. Applications of Aluminum Compounds | 铝化合物的应用

Al₂O₃:

  • Abrasive (磨料)
  • Refractory material (耐火材料)
  • Catalyst (催化剂) support (催化剂载体)
  • Gemstones (ruby, sapphire) (宝石（红宝石、蓝宝石）)

Al(OH)₃:

  • Antacid medication (抗酸药)
  • Flame retardant (阻燃剂)
  • Water treatment (水处理)

Al₂(SO₄)₃:

  • Water purification (净水)
  • Paper sizing (纸张施胶)
  • Textile dyeing (纺织染色)
