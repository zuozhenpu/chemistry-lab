Part 1: Preparation and Properties of Sulfur Dioxide | 二氧化硫的制备与性质

Materials and Equipment | 材料与仪器

Chemicals | 化学药品:

  • Concentrated sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄) | 浓硫酸
  • Copper turnings or wire | 铜屑或铜丝
  • Sodium hydroxide solution (NaOH) soaked cotton | 浸泡过氢氧化钠溶液的棉花
  • Fuchsin (Magenta) solution | 品红溶液
  • pH paper (wet) | 湿润的pH试纸

Equipment | 实验仪器:

  • Test tubes | 试管
  • Single-hole runner stopper with delivery tube | 带导管的单孔橡胶塞
  • Alcohol lamp | 酒精灯
  • Test tube holder | 试管夹
  • Stand with clamps (optional) | 铁架台（可选）

Procedure | 操作步骤

Step 1: Gas Generation | 气体发生

  • Place copper turnings in a dry test tube.
  • 在干燥试管中放入铜屑。
  • Add 2-3 mL of concentrated H₂SO₄.
  • 加入2-3毫升浓硫酸。
  • CAUTION: Concentrated acid is corrosive!
  • 注意：浓酸有腐蚀性！

Step 2: Setup and Heating | 装置与加热

  • Insert the stopper with delivery tube.
  • 塞上带导管的橡胶塞。
  • Prepare a second test tube with 2 mL of Fuchsin (Magenta) solution.
  • 准备另一支试管，装入2毫升品红溶液。
  • Gently heat the H₂SO₄/Cu mixture.
  • 轻轻加热硫酸/铜混合物。
  • ⚠️ Safety Warning: Perform in a fume hood! SO₂ is toxic!
  • ⚠️ 安全警告：在通风橱中进行！SO₂有毒！

Step 3: Properties Testing | 性质测试

  • Acidity: Hold a piece of wet pH paper near the tube outlet (before inserting into solution). Observe color change.
  • 酸性：将湿润的pH试纸靠近导管口（插入溶液前）。观察颜色变化。
  • Bleaching: Insert the delivery tube into the Fuchsin solution. Observe color change.
  • 漂白性：将导管插入品红溶液中。观察颜色变化。
  • Reversibility: After the Fuchsin fades, remove the tube and heat the Fuchsin solution gently. Observe if color returns.
  • 可逆性：品红褪色后，取出导管，轻轻加热品红溶液。观察颜色是否恢复。

Step 4: Disposal | 废液处理

  • Use NaOH-soaked cotton to plug the reaction tube after finishing to absorb remaining gas.
  • 实验结束后，用浸泡过NaOH的棉花堵住反应试管口以吸收剩余气体。

Part 2: Dehydrating Property of Concentrated Sulfuric Acid | 浓硫酸的脱水性

  • Small beaker (50 mL) | 小烧杯（50毫升）

Part 3: Identification of Sulfate Ions | 硫酸根离子的检验

Materials and Equipment | 材料与仪器

Chemicals | 化学药品:

  • Sodium sulfate solution (Na₂SO₄) | 硫酸钠溶液

  • Barium chloride solution (BaCl₂) | 氯化钡溶液
  • Unknown solution (containing carbonate or sulfate) | 未知溶液（含碳酸根或硫酸根）

Equipment | 实验仪器:

  • Test tubes | 试管
  • Dropper | 滴管

Procedure | 操作步骤

Step 1: Standard Test | 标准测试

  • Add 2 mL of Na₂SO₄ solution to a test tube.
  • 向试管中加入2毫升Na₂SO₄溶液。
  • Add few drops of dilute HCl (to acidify).
  • 加入几滴稀盐酸（酸化）。
  • Add few drops of BaCl₂ solution.
  • 加入几滴BaCl₂溶液。
  • Observe precipitate.
  • 观察沉淀。

Step 2: Exclusion of Interference | 排除干扰

  • Take a small amount of Na₂CO₃ solution in another tube.
  • 在另一支试管中取少量Na₂CO₃溶液。
  • Add BaCl₂ (white precipitate forms).
  • 加入BaCl₂（生成白色沉淀）。
  • Add dilute HCl. Observe if it dissolves.
  • 加入稀盐酸。观察是否溶解。

Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Hydrogen Sulfide (H₂S) | 硫化氢

Properties | 性质:

  • Colorless gas (无色气体)
  • Rotten egg odor (臭鸡蛋气味)
  • Highly toxic (剧毒)
  • Soluble (可溶) in water (易溶于水)

Acidic Properties | 酸性:


H₂S ⇌ H⁺ + HS⁻ (weak acid)
H₂S + 2NaOH → Na₂S + 2H₂O

Reducing Properties | 还原性:


H₂S + Cl₂ → 2HCl + S↓
H₂S + Br₂ → 2HBr + S↓

Precipitation Reactions | 沉淀反应:


H₂S + CuSO₄ → CuS↓ (black) + H₂SO₄
H₂S + Pb(NO₃)₂ → PbS↓ (black) + 2HNO₃

2. Sulfurous Acid (H₂SO₃) | 亚硫酸

Formation | 生成:


SO₂ + H₂O ⇌ H₂SO₃
  • Unstable, exists only in solution (不稳定，仅存在于溶液中)

Acidic Properties | 酸性:


H₂SO₃ + 2NaOH → Na₂SO₃ + 2H₂O
H₂SO₃ + NaOH → NaHSO₃ + H₂O

Reducing Properties | 还原性:


H₂SO₃ + Cl₂ + H₂O → H₂SO₄ + 2HCl

3. Sulfuric Acid (H₂SO₄) | 硫酸

Properties | 性质:

  • Colorless, oily liquid (无色油状液体)
  • Highly corrosive (强腐蚀性)
  • Strong acid (强酸)
  • Hygroscopic (吸水性)

Dilute H₂SO₄ Reactions | 稀硫酸反应:

With Metals | 与金属:


Zn + H₂SO₄(dilute) → ZnSO₄ + H₂↑
Fe + H₂SO₄(dilute) → FeSO₄ + H₂↑

With Bases | 与碱:


H₂SO₄ + 2NaOH → Na₂SO₄ + 2H₂O
H₂SO₄ + Cu(OH)₂ → CuSO₄ + 2H₂O

With Metal Oxides | 与金属氧化物:


H₂SO₄ + CuO → CuSO₄ + H₂O

Concentrated H₂SO₄ Special Properties | 浓硫酸特殊性质:

1. Dehydration | 脱水性:


C₁₂H₂₂O₁₁ --浓H₂SO₄--> 12C + 11H₂O
  • Chars organic materials (使有机物炭化)

2. Oxidizing Property | 氧化性:


Cu + 2H₂SO₄(conc) --Δ--> CuSO₄ + SO₂↑ + 2H₂O
C + 2H₂SO₄(conc) --Δ--> CO₂↑ + 2SO₂↑ + 2H₂O

3. Hygroscopic | 吸水性:

  • Absorbs moisture from air (从空气中吸收水分)
  • Used as desiccant (用作干燥剂)

4. Sulfites and Sulfates | 亚硫酸盐和硫酸盐

Sodium Sulfite (Na₂SO₃) | 亚硫酸钠:


2NaOH + SO₂ → Na₂SO₃ + H₂O
  • Reducing agent (还原剂)
  • Food preservative (食品防腐剂)

Sodium Sulfate (Na₂SO₄) | 硫酸钠:


2NaOH + H₂SO₄ → Na₂SO₄ + 2H₂O
  • Glauber's salt: Na₂SO₄·10H₂O (芒硝)
  • Used in glass and detergent production (用于玻璃和洗涤剂生产)

Barium Sulfate (BaSO₄) | 硫酸钡:


BaCl₂ + H₂SO₄ → BaSO₄↓ + 2HCl
  • White precipitate (白色沉淀)
  • Insoluble (不溶) in acids (不溶于酸)
  • Used to test for SO₄²⁻ ions (用于检验SO₄²⁻离子)
  • Medical imaging (医学成像)

5. Industrial Production of H₂SO₄ | 硫酸的工业生产

Contact Process | 接触法:

Step 1: Burn sulfur | 燃烧硫:


S + O₂ → SO₂

Step 2: Oxidation | 氧化:


2SO₂ + O₂ --V₂O₅催化剂,Δ--> 2SO₃

Step 3: Absorption | 吸收:


SO₃ + H₂SO₄(conc) → H₂S₂O₇ (oleum)
H₂S₂O₇ + H₂O → 2H₂SO₄

6. Applications | 应用

H₂SO₄:

  • Fertilizer production (肥料生产)
  • Petroleum refining (石油精炼)
  • Metal processing (金属加工)
  • Battery acid (电池酸)
  • Chemical synthesis (化学合成)
