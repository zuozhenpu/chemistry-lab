Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Physical Properties of Sodium | 钠的物理性质

Appearance | 外观:

  • Soft, silvery-white metal (软的银白色金属)
  • Can be cut with a knife (可以用刀切割)
  • Freshly cut surface is shiny but tarnishes quickly in air (新切面有光泽但在空气中迅速失去光泽)

Density | 密度:

  • 0.97 g/cm³ (less dense than water) (比水密度小)
  • This explains why sodium floats on water (这解释了为什么钠浮在水面上)

Melting Point | 熔点:

  • 97.8°C (relatively low for a metal) (对于金属来说相对较低)

Conductivity | 导电性:

  • Good conductor of heat and electricity (良好的热和电导体)
  • Used in some electrical applications (用于某些电气应用)

2. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Reaction with Oxygen | 与氧气反应

At Room Temperature | 室温下:


4Na + O₂ → 2Na₂O (sodium oxide, white)

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Sodium tarnishes quickly in air (钠在空气中迅速失去光泽)
  • Forms a white coating of Na₂O (形成白色Na₂O涂层)
  • This is why sodium must be stored under oil (这就是为什么钠必须储存在油中)

When Heated | 加热时:


2Na + O₂ --Δ--> Na₂O₂ (sodium peroxide, pale yellow)

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Vigorous reaction with bright yellow flame (剧烈反应，明亮的黄色火焰)
  • Forms pale yellow sodium peroxide (形成淡黄色过氧化钠)
  • Yellow flame color is characteristic of sodium (黄色火焰是钠的特征)

(2) Reaction with Water | 与水反应

Chemical Equation | 化学方程式:


2Na + 2H₂O → 2NaOH + H₂↑

Detailed Observations Explained | 详细观察解释:

1. Sodium floats | 钠浮在水面:

  • - Density of Na (0.97 g/cm³) < density of H₂O (1.00 g/cm³)
  • - 钠的密度（0.97克/立方厘米）< 水的密度（1.00克/立方厘米）

2. Melts into a ball | 熔化成球:

  • - Reaction is highly exothermic (反应强烈放热)
  • - Heat released > melting point of Na (97.8°C) (释放的热 > 钠的熔点（97.8°C）)
  • - Surface tension forms spherical shape (表面张力形成球形)

3. Moves rapidly on surface | 在表面快速移动:

  • - H₂ gas pushes sodium around (H₂气体推动钠移动)
  • - Like a tiny hovercraft (像一个小型气垫船)

4. Hissing sound | 嘶嘶声:

  • - Rapid release of H₂ gas (H₂气体快速释放)

5. Solution becomes alkaline | 溶液变碱性:

  • - NaOH formed is a strong base (生成的NaOH是强碱)
  • - pH > 7 (pH > 7)
  • - Phenolphthalein indicator turns pink (酚酞指示剂变红)

(3) Reaction with Acids | 与酸反应

With Hydrochloric Acid | 与盐酸反应:


2Na + 2HCl → 2NaCl + H₂↑

With Sulfuric Acid | 与硫酸反应:


2Na + H₂SO₄(dilute) → Na₂SO₄ + H₂↑

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Even MORE vigorous than reaction with water (比与水反应更剧烈)
  • Rapid evolution of H₂ gas (H₂气体快速放出)
  • May ignite the hydrogen (可能点燃氢气)
  • DANGER: Never add large pieces of sodium to acid! (危险：绝不可将大块钠加入酸中！)

(4) Reaction with Salt Solutions | 与盐溶液反应

Important Principle | 重要原理:

Sodium reacts with WATER first, NOT directly with the salt!

钠先与水反应，不是直接与盐反应！

Example: Sodium with CuSO₄ Solution | 示例：钠与CuSO₄溶液反应:


Step 1: 2Na + 2H₂O → 2NaOH + H₂↑
Step 2: 2NaOH + CuSO₄ → Cu(OH)₂↓ + Na₂SO₄

Overall: 2Na + 2H₂O + CuSO₄ → Cu(OH)₂↓ + Na₂SO₄ + H₂↑

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Blue precipitate of Cu(OH)₂ forms (生成蓝色Cu(OH)₂沉淀)
  • NO copper metal produced! (不产生铜单质！)
  • Common misconception: Na does NOT displace Cu from CuSO₄ in aqueous solution
  • 常见误解：在水溶液中Na不会从CuSO₄中置换出Cu

Why? | 为什么？

  • Sodium is too reactive (钠太活泼)
  • It reacts with water before it can react with the salt (它在与盐反应之前就与水反应了)

3. Activity of Sodium | 钠的活性

Position in Activity Series | 在活动性顺序中的位置:


K > Na > Ca > Mg > Al > Zn > Fe > ... > H > Cu > Ag > Au

Implications | 意义:

  • Sodium is very reactive (钠非常活泼)
  • Cannot exist freely in nature (不能在自然界中自由存在)
  • Always found in compounds (总是以化合物形式存在)
  • Must be stored under oil or kerosene (必须储存在油或煤油中)

4. Storage and Safety | 储存与安全

Storage Requirements | 储存要求:

  • Must be stored under kerosene or mineral oil (必须储存在煤油或矿物油中)
  • Prevents contact with air (oxygen and moisture) (防止与空气接触（氧气和水分）)
  • Store in cool, dry place (储存在阴凉干燥处)

Safety Precautions | 安全预防措施:

1. Never touch with bare hands | 绝不可徒手接触

  • - Use forceps or tongs (使用镊子或钳子)
  • - Sodium can burn skin (钠会灼伤皮肤)

2. Keep away from water | 远离水

  • - Violent reaction (剧烈反应)
  • - May ignite hydrogen (可能点燃氢气)

3. Proper disposal | 正确处理

  • - Dispose of waste sodium in ethanol, NEVER in water (废钠放入乙醇处理，绝不可放入水中)
  • - Ethanol reacts more slowly and safely (乙醇反应更慢更安全)
    • 
   2Na + 2C₂H₅OH → 2C₂H₅ONa + H₂↑

4. Fire safety | 火灾安全

  • - Sodium fires CANNOT be extinguished with water (钠火灾不能用水扑灭)
  • - Use dry sand or special metal fire extinguishers (使用干沙或特殊金属灭火器)

5. Uses of Sodium | 钠的用途

1. Sodium-vapor Lamps | 钠蒸气灯

  • Yellow street lights (黄色路灯)
  • High efficiency (高效率)
  • Characteristic yellow glow from sodium emission (钠发射的特征黄光)

2. Nuclear Reactors | 核反应堆

  • Liquid sodium as heat transfer medium (液态钠作为传热介质)
  • Good thermal conductivity (良好的导热性)
  • High boiling point (680°C) (高沸点（680°C）)

3. Chemical Production | 化工生产

  • Production of sodium compounds (NaOH, Na₂CO₃, etc.) (生产钠化合物（NaOH、Na₂CO₃等）)
  • Reducing agent in metallurgy (冶金中的还原剂)
  • Production of titanium from TiCl₄ (从TiCl₄生产钛)

4. Sodium-Sulfur Batteries | 钠硫电池

  • Energy storage systems (能量储存系统)
  • High energy density (高能量密度)
  • Used in grid energy storage (用于电网能量储存)

I. Key Properties | 关键性质

Physical Properties | 物理性质

1. **Appearance | 外观**: Silvery-white metal, metallic luster (银白色金属，有金属光泽).

2. **Hardness | 硬度**: Soft, can be cut with a knife (质软，可用小刀切割).

3. **Density | 密度**: $\rho(Na) = 0.97 \text{ g/cm}^3$, $\rho(H_2O) > \rho(Na) > \rho(kerosene)$ (密度比水小，比煤油大).

4. **Melting Point | 熔点**: Low ($97.8^\circ\text{C}$), melts into a silvery ball during reaction with water (熔点低，与水反应时熔化成小球).

5. **Conductivity | 导电性**: Good conductor of electricity and heat (良好的导电、导热性).

Chemical Properties | 化学性质 (Strong Reducibility | 强还原性)

Sodium is a very active metal and acts as a strong reducing agent in reactions. The most common valence is +1.

钠是非常活泼的金属，在反应中表现出强还原性。常见化合价为+1价。

II. Important Reactions | 重要反应 (Gaokao Focus | 高考重点)

1. Reaction with Non-metals | 与非金属反应

  • **With Oxygen at Room Temperature | 常温下与氧气反应**:

$$4Na + O_2 \rightarrow 2Na_2O$$

(White solid formed | 生成白色固体)

  • **With Oxygen under Heating | 加热时与氧气反应**:

$$2Na + O_2 \xrightarrow{\Delta} Na_2O_2$$

(Pale yellow solid formed, stable peroxide | 生成淡黄色固体过氧化钠，较稳定)

Key Point | 考点: Conditions determine the product (temperature dependent). 条件决定产物（温度不同产物不同）。

2. Reaction with Water | 与水反应 (High Frequency Exam Point | 高频考点)

  • **Chemical Equation | 化学方程式**:

$$2Na + 2H_2O \rightarrow 2NaOH + H_2\uparrow$$

  • **Ionic Equation | 离子方程式**:

$$2Na + 2H_2O \rightarrow 2Na^+ + 2OH^- + H_2\uparrow$$

  • **Phenomena Summary | 现象总结 “Five Characters” (五字诀)**:
  • - **Float (浮)**: Density is less than water (密度小于水).
  • - **Melt (熔)**: Exothermic reaction, low melting point (反应放热，熔点低).
  • - **Swim (游)**: Gas generation pushes sodium (产生气体推动).
  • - **Hiss (响)**: Vigorous reaction (剧烈反应).
  • - **Red (红)**: Alkali forms, phenolphthalein turns red (生成碱，酚酞变红).

3. Reaction with Salt Solutions | 与盐溶液反应

  • **Principle | 原理**: Sodium reacts with water *first*, then the generated NaOH reacts with the salt. (钠先与水反应，生成的碱再与盐反应)。
  • **Example (with $CuSO_4$) | 示例**:

1. $2Na + 2H_2O \rightarrow 2NaOH + H_2\uparrow$

2. $2NaOH + CuSO_4 \rightarrow Cu(OH)_2\downarrow + Na_2SO_4$

Total Equation | 总反应:

$$2Na + 2H_2O + CuSO_4 \rightarrow Cu(OH)_2\downarrow + Na_2SO_4 + H_2\uparrow$$

Note: Sodium does not displace Copper from solution directly! (注意：钠不能直接从溶液中置换出铜！)

III. Applications | 应用

1. **Preparation of other metals | 制取其他金属**: Using strong reducibility to displace metals from molten salts (e.g., $Ti$ from $TiCl_4$). (利用强还原性，从熔融盐中置换金属)。

$$4Na + TiCl_4 \text{ (molten)} \xrightarrow{\text{Ar}} Ti + 4NaCl$$

2. **High-pressure Sodium Lamps | 高压钠灯**: Strong fog penetrability, used for street lighting (透雾性强，用于路灯).

3. **Nuclear Reactor Coolant | 核反应堆导热剂**: Na-K alloy is liquid at room temperature with good thermal conductivity (钠钾合金常温下为液态，导热性好).
