Experimental Principles | 实验原理

Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Physical Properties of Iron | 铁的物理性质

Appearance | 外观:

  • Silvery-gray metal with metallic luster (银灰色金属，具有金属光泽)
  • Hard and strong (坚硬而强韧)
  • Malleable and ductile (可锻可延展)

Density | 密度:

  • 7.87 g/cm³ (relatively high density) (相对较高的密度)

Melting Point | 熔点:

  • 1538°C (high melting point) (高熔点)

Magnetic Properties | 磁性:

  • Ferromagnetic (铁磁性)
  • Can be magnetized (可以被磁化)
  • One of only three ferromagnetic elements at room temperature (Fe, Co, Ni)
  • 室温下仅有的三种铁磁性元素之一（Fe、Co、Ni）

2. Chemical Properties | 化学性质

(1) Reaction with Oxygen | 与氧气反应

In Dry Air at Room Temperature | 在室温干燥空气中:

  • Iron is relatively stable (铁相对稳定)
  • Forms a thin protective oxide layer (形成薄的保护性氧化层)

In Moist Air | 在潮湿空气中:


4Fe + 3O₂ + 6H₂O → 4Fe(OH)₃
  • Rust (锈)ing occurs (生锈发生)
  • Forms reddish-brown rust (Fe₂O₃·xH₂O) (形成红棕色铁锈)

When Heated | 加热时:


3Fe + 2O₂ --Δ--> Fe₃O₄ (black iron oxide)
  • Vigorous reaction at high temperature (高温下剧烈反应)
  • Forms black Fe₃O₄ (magnetic iron oxide) (形成黑色Fe₃O₄（磁性氧化铁）)

(2) Reaction with Water/Steam | 与水/水蒸气反应

  • Very slow reaction (非常缓慢的反应)
  • Negligible under normal conditions (正常条件下可忽略不计)

With Steam | 与水蒸气:


3Fe + 4H₂O(g) --Δ--> Fe₃O₄ + 4H₂↑
  • Reaction occurs at high temperature (高温下发生反应)
  • Produces hydrogen gas (产生氢气)

(3) Reaction with Acids | 与酸反应


Fe + 2HCl → FeCl₂ + H₂↑
  • Produces light green Fe²⁺ solution (产生浅绿色Fe²⁺溶液)
  • Hydrogen gas evolved (放出氢气)

With Dilute Sulfuric Acid | 与稀硫酸:


Fe + H₂SO₄(dilute) → FeSO₄ + H₂↑
  • Similar to reaction with HCl (与HCl反应类似)

With Concentrated Sulfuric Acid (Cold) | 与浓硫酸（冷）:

  • Iron is passivated (铁被钝化)
  • Forms protective oxide layer (形成保护性氧化层)
  • NO reaction at room temperature (室温下不反应)

2Fe + 6H₂SO₄(conc, hot) → Fe₂(SO₄)₃ + 3SO₂↑ + 6H₂O
  • Produces Fe³⁺ ions (产生Fe³⁺离子)
  • SO₂ gas evolved (放出SO₂气体)

With Nitric Acid | 与硝酸:

Dilute HNO₃ | 稀硝酸:


3Fe + 8HNO₃(dilute) → 3Fe(NO₃)₂ + 2NO↑ + 4H₂O

Concentrated HNO₃ (Cold) | 浓硝酸（冷）:

  • Iron is passivated (铁被钝化)
  • Forms protective oxide layer (形成保护性氧化层)

With Copper Sulfate Solution | 与硫酸铜溶液:


Fe + CuSO₄ → FeSO₄ + Cu
  • Iron displaces copper (铁置换出铜)
  • Red-brown copper deposits on iron (红棕色铜沉积在铁上)
  • Solution color changes from blue to light green (溶液颜色从蓝色变为浅绿色)

3. Variable Valence of Iron | 铁的变价

Two Common Oxidation States | 两种常见氧化态:

Fe²⁺ (Ferrous) | 亚铁离子:

  • Light green in solution (溶液中呈浅绿色)
  • Reducing agent (还原剂)
  • Can be oxidized to Fe³⁺ (可被氧化成Fe³⁺)

Fe³⁺ (Ferric) | 铁离子:

  • Yellow-brown in solution (溶液中呈黄棕色)
  • Oxidizing agent (氧化剂)
  • Can be reduced to Fe²⁺ (可被还原成Fe²⁺)

Interconversion | 相互转化:

Fe²⁺ → Fe³⁺ (Oxidation | 氧化):


2Fe²⁺ + Cl₂ → 2Fe³⁺ + 2Cl⁻

Fe³⁺ → Fe²⁺ (Reduction | 还原):


2Fe³⁺ + Fe → 3Fe²⁺

4. Iron Oxides | 铁的氧化物

FeO (Iron(II) Oxide) | 氧化亚铁:

  • Black solid (黑色固体)
  • Basic (碱性) oxide (碱性氧化物)
  • Unstable, easily oxidized to Fe₃O₄ (不稳定，易被氧化成Fe₃O₄)

Fe₂O₃ (Iron(III) Oxide) | 三氧化二铁:

  • Red-brown solid (红棕色固体)
  • Main component of rust (铁锈的主要成分)
  • Amphoteric (两性) oxide (两性氧化物)

Fe₃O₄ (Iron(II,III) Oxide) | 四氧化三铁:

  • Black solid (黑色固体)
  • Magnetic (有磁性)
  • Mixed oxide: FeO·Fe₂O₃ (混合氧化物：FeO·Fe₂O₃)

5. Uses of Iron | 铁的用途

1. Construction and Manufacturing | 建筑和制造:

  • Steel production (钢铁生产)
  • Buildings, bridges, vehicles (建筑物、桥梁、车辆)
  • Most widely used metal (使用最广泛的金属)

2. Alloys | 合金:

  • Steel (iron + carbon) (钢（铁+碳）)
  • Stainless steel (iron + chromium + nickel) (不锈钢（铁+铬+镍）)
  • Cast iron (铸铁)

3. Magnets | 磁铁:

  • Permanent magnets (永久磁铁)
  • Electromagnets (电磁铁)
  • Magnetic storage media (磁存储介质)

4. Chemical Industry | 化工:

  • Catalyst (催化剂)s (催化剂)
  • Production of iron compounds (生产铁化合物)

6. Prevention of Rusting | 防锈方法

1. Protective Coatings | 保护涂层:

  • Paint (油漆)
  • Oil or grease (油或油脂)
  • Plastic coating (塑料涂层)

2. Galvanization | 镀锌:

  • Coating with zinc (镀锌)
  • Zinc acts as sacrificial anode (锌作为牺牲阳极)

3. Alloying | 合金化:

  • Stainless steel (contains chromium) (不锈钢（含铬）)
  • Chromium forms protective oxide layer (铬形成保护性氧化层)

4. Cathodic Protection | 阴极保护:

  • Attach more reactive metal (连接更活泼的金属)
  • Protects iron from oxidation (保护铁不被氧化)
