Now that you have completed the experimental procedures and made observations, let's understand the underlying principles.

现在你已经完成了实验操作并进行了观察，让我们来理解其背后的原理。

1. Molecular Structure of Acetic Acid | 乙酸的分子结构

Molecular Formula | 分子式: CH₃COOH

Structural Formula | 结构式:


    H
    |
H - C - C = O
    |   ||
    H   O - H

Functional Group | 官能团: Carboxyl group (-COOH) | 羧基

Key Structural Features | 关键结构特征:

  • Contains both carbonyl (C=O) and hydroxyl (-OH) groups
  • 同时含有羰基（C=O）和羟基（-OH）
  • The hydrogen of the -OH group can be partially ionized
  • -OH基团中的氢可以部分电离
  • Weak acid due to incomplete dissociation
  • 由于不完全电离而是弱酸

2. Physical Properties | 物理性质

Appearance | 外观:

  • Colorless liquid below 16.6°C, becomes "glacial" (ice-like)
  • 16.6°C以下为无色液体，变成"冰状"
  • Strong, pungent, vinegar-like odor
  • 强烈、刺激性、醋味

Density | 密度:

  • 1.05 g/cm³ at 20°C (denser than water)
  • 20°C时为1.05克/立方厘米（比水密度大）

Melting Point | 熔点:

  • 16.6°C (forms "glacial" acetic acid below this temperature)
  • 16.6°C（低于此温度形成"冰醋酸"）

Boiling Point | 沸点:

  • 118°C (higher than water due to hydrogen bonding)
  • 118°C（由于氢键作用比水高）

Solubility | 溶解性:

  • Miscible with water in all proportions
  • 与水以任意比例互溶
  • Good solvent for many organic and inorganic substances
  • 许多有机和无机物质的良好溶剂

3. Acidic Properties | 酸性性质

(1) Weak Acid Nature | 弱酸性质

Ionization Equation | 电离方程式:


CH₃COOH ⇌ CH₃COO⁻ + H⁺

Acid Dissociation Constant | 酸性电离常数:

  • Ka = 1.8 × 10⁻⁵ at 25°C
  • 25°C时Ka = 1.8 × 10⁻⁵
  • Only about 1% of acetic acid molecules ionize in 0.1M solution
  • 在0.1M溶液中只有约1%的乙酸分子电离

(2) Typical Acid Reactions | 典型酸反应

With Carbonates | 与碳酸盐反应:


2CH₃COOH + Na₂CO₃ → 2CH₃COONa + H₂O + CO₂↑

With Active Metals | 与活泼金属反应:


2CH₃COOH + 2Na → 2CH₃COONa + H₂↑

4. Esterification Reaction | 酯化反应

General Reaction | 一般反应:


R-COOH + R'-OH ⇌ R-COO-R' + H₂O

Specific Example (acetic acid + ethanol) | 具体例子（乙酸 + 乙醇）:


CH₃COOH + C₂H₅OH ⇌ CH₃COOC₂H₅ + H₂O
                    (ethyl acetate)

Reaction Conditions | 反应条件:

  • Catalyst (催化剂): Concentrated H₂SO₄
  • Temperature: 60-70°C (water bath)
  • Time: 10-15 minutes

Mechanism | 机理:

  • "Acid loses -OH, alcohol loses -H"
  • "酸脱羟基，醇脱氢"
  • The oxygen in the ester comes from the alcohol, not the acid
  • 酯中的氧来自醇，而非酸

Observations Explained | 观察解释:

  • Fruity odor indicates ester formation (ethyl acetate)
  • 果香味表明酯的生成（乙酸乙酯）
  • Oily layer on top (ester is less dense than water and immiscible)
  • 顶部油层（酯比水密度小且不互溶）
  • Bubbles when added to Na₂CO₃ (CO₂ from neutralization of excess acid)
  • 加入Na₂CO₃时产生气泡（中和过量酸产生的CO₂）

5. Comparison with Mineral Acids | 与无机酸的比较

| Property | Acetic Acid | HCl | H₂SO₄ |

|----------|-------------|-----|--------|

| Type | Organic weak acid | Mineral strong acid | Mineral strong acid |

| Strength | Weak (Ka = 1.8 × 10⁻⁵) | Strong (complete ionization) | Strong (complete ionization) |

| Conductivity | Poor | Good | Excellent |

| pH (0.1M) | ~2.9 | ~1.0 | ~0.8 |

| Reaction with metals | Moderate | Vigorous | Very vigorous |

| Oxidizing ability | None | None | Strong (conc.) |

6. Industrial Production of Acetic Acid | 乙酸的工业生产

(a) Fermentation Method (Traditional) | 发酵法（传统）

Process | 过程:

  • Ethanol oxidation by Acetobacter bacteria
  • 乙醇被醋酸菌氧化
    • 
C₂H₅OH + O₂ --bacteria--> CH₃COOH + H₂O

Applications | 应用:

  • Vinegar production (4-8% acetic acid)
  • 醋的生产（4-8%乙酸）

(b) Methanol Carbonylation (Modern) | 甲醇羰基化法（现代）

Monsanto Process | 孟山都法:


CH₃OH + CO --Rh/Ir catalyst, 150-200°C, 30-60 atm--> CH₃COOH

Advantages | 优点:

  • High yield (>99%)
  • 高产率（>99%）
  • Uses inexpensive raw materials
  • 使用廉价原料
  • Environmentally friendly
  • 环境友好

Knowledge Summary | 知识点梳理

I. Structure and Properties | 结构与性质

Molecular Structure | 分子结构:

  • Formula: CH₃COOH
  • 分子式：CH₃COOH
  • Functional group: Carboxyl (-COOH)
  • 官能团：羧基（-COOH）
  • Contains carbonyl and hydroxyl groups
  • 含有羰基和羟基

Physical Properties | 物理性质:

  • Colorless liquid with pungent odor
  • 无色液体，刺激性气味
  • Forms "glacial" solid below 16.6°C
  • 16.6°C以下形成"冰状"固体
  • Miscible with water
  • 与水互溶

II. Chemical Behavior | 化学性质

Acidic Nature | 酸性:

  • Weak acid (Ka = 1.8 × 10⁻⁵)
  • 弱酸（Ka = 1.8 × 10⁻⁵）
  • Partial ionization
  • 部分电离
  • pH of 0.1M solution ~2.9
  • 0.1M溶液的pH约2.9

Typical Reactions | 典型反应:

  • With indicators (turns blue litmus red)
  • 与指示剂反应（使蓝石蕊变红）
  • With carbonates (produces CO₂)
  • 与碳酸盐反应（产生CO₂）
  • With metals (produces H₂)
  • 与金属反应（产生H₂）

III. Important Reactions | 重要反应

Esterification | 酯化:

  • React with alcohols to form esters
  • 与醇反应生成酯
  • Reaction: CH₃COOH + R-OH → CH₃COOR + H₂O
  • Requires acid catalyst and heat
  • 需要酸催化剂和加热

IV. Key Concepts | 重要概念

"Glacial" Acetic Acid | "冰醋酸":

  • Pure acetic acid forms ice-like crystals below 16.6°C
  • 纯乙酸在16.6°C以下形成冰状晶体
  • Name derives from appearance
  • 名称来源于外观

Weak Acid Behavior | 弱酸行为:

  • Incomplete dissociation in water
  • 在水中不完全电离
  • Equilibrium between molecules and ions
  • 分子和离子之间的平衡
  • Less than 5% ionization in dilute solutions
  • 稀溶液中电离度小于5%
